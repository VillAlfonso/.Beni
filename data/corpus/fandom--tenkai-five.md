Tenkai Five (Hebrew: חמישיית טנקאיי, Spanish: Los cinco Tenkai, Turkish: Tenkai Beşlisi, French: Les cinq Tenkai, German: Tenkai Fünf) is the first webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins. It was first streamed in the United States on September 17, 2013 on Tenkai Knights' official YouTube channel.

## Plot

The Tenkai Knights are introduced in a stadium by the Guardians. Vilius challenges the Knights to determine who is the best. As they were fighting, the Guardians stop the battle and determine it as a draw and Vilius walks away.
