Ebay (Japanese: イーベイ, Ībei, Chinese: 電子灣, Diànzǐwān, Korean: 이베이, Ibei), stylized as eBay, is an online worldwide retailing company that was founded on September 3, 1995 by Pierre Omidyar.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

eBay has many items of the Tenkai Knights product line available to purchase.
