The Tenkai Knights Mascot Collection (Japanese: テンカイナイトマスコットコレクション, Tenkai Naito Masukotto Korekushon, Korean: 텐카이 나이트 마스코트 컬렉션, Tenkai Naiteu Maseukoteu Keollegsyeon) is a toy line, made by Happinet, that was released in Japan on August 7, 2014. The available characters are Braven, Tributon, Valorn, Lydendor, and Vilius. Buying one of the figures costs 300円. A fan on Twitter, known as Ank, has made many mascot toys of characters. These characters aren't officially mascots, but it's possible. Kurokishi is now official (Dromus).
