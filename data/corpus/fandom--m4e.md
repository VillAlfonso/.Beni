M4E, stylized as m4e, and short for Made for Entertainment, is a media company that specifies on children and family entertainment content. It was founded in 2003.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

Viz Media Europe gave m4e the job of licensing agent in the GAS countries (Germany, Austria, and (German-speaking) Switzerland) for Tenkai Knights in Europe.
