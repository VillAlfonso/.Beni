GO! (originally to be named GO!99) is an Australian entertainment multichannel, owned by the Nine Entertainment Company, that features live shows and animated shows. Some live shows like TMZ, The Voice, and The Big Bang Theory are broadcasted on that channel. The name was revealed as GO!99 on April 14, 2009 by TV Tonight and was comfirmed on July 15, 2009 as GO!. On August 5, 2009, GO! was put on air, sponsoring a promo loop. GO! was broadcasting on August 9, 2009 by the Nine Network. This channel features a few anime shows like Yu-Gi-Oh! And Yo-Kai watch! and is for those who are from 14 to 39 years old.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

<!--ep:39-->
On April 07, 2014, the first episode of Tenkai Knights aired on GO!. New episodes are broadcasted everyday and there were some times where GO! took some 3-day breaks. After the release of "Welcome to the Jungle", GO! had to take at least a two month break until July 28, 2014, where new episodes will continue from there.
