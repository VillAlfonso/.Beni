Sears (Japanese: シアーズ, Shiāzu, Korean: 시어스, Sieoseu) is an American retailing company that was founded in 1886 by Alvah Curtis Roebuck and Richard Warren Sears. There are Sears stores available in the U.S., Canada, and Mexico.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

Sears is one of the few retail stores left that still sells the Tenkai Knights toys. You can find many of the products on the Sears site.
