The Tenkai Knights Perfect Guidebook (Korean: 텐카이나이트 퍼펙트 가이드북, Tenkai Naiteu Peopegteu Gaideubug), also called the Tenkai Knights Leaflet (Korean: 텐카이나이트 리플렛, Tenkai Naiteu Lipeulles), is a mini booklet that was released under the Korean Tenkai Knights product line on March 17, 2015.
