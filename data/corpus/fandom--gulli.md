Gulli (formely called Gulliver in 2004 and also known as Cyberbot), owned by Lagardère Active, is a French broadcast channel aimed at the young and the family and is available by cable, digital terrestrial television (DTT), internet protocol television (IPTV), satellite, and the web. The channel launched on Novenber 18, 2005 and is also available to watch in Russia. Gulli contains many programs for children like Gulli d'Or, Boing, Labeled Funny, Code Adventure, and Girl Power. The channel broadcasts anime shows like Bakugan, Dinosaur King, Inazuma Eleven, Beyblade, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and Astro Boy.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

As revealed by Viz Media, Gulli will air Tenkai Knights in the fall of 2014. However, it is shown that Gulli posted on their website the first episode of Tenkai Knights.
