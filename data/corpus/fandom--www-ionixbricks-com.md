www.ionixbricks.com is the official website for toys involved in the Ionix toy line. It was launched in late 2013.

## Media

The site features webisodes of Tenkai Knights: Origins and games of Tenkai Knights. It contains a list of products of the Ionix toy line, which currently are Dragons, Tenkai Knights, and Pokémon, and instructions to building most of the toys. There is also a simulator that shows how some of the toys can be opened, info about the Ionix toy line, and FAQ's to answer anyone's questions, regarding with the toys, shows, and the site. The site is currently available in English, Italian, Hebrew, Spanish, Dutch, Turkish, French, and German.
