## Facts

North America: }}

Elemental Titan/Hyper DX Packs are boxes that contain three parts: a core brick, pieces to build the figure, and a booklet towards building the figure. These are released in the Western world, known as Elemental Titan Packs, and in Japan, known as Hyper DX Packs. In the Korean region, they are known as Sigma Mode Packs.
