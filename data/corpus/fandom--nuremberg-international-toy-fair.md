The Nuremberg International Toy Fair, also called the Spielwarenmesse International Toy Fair Nürnberg, or simply Spielwarenmesse, is an annual event held in Nuremberg, Germany. It covers twelve different product groups and is the largest international trade fair of its kind in the world. Organized by Spielwarenmesse eG, invited guests include the press and professionals of the toy industry.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

At the Nuremberg International Toy Fair in 2014, many toys from Tenkai Knights were exhibited, including basic figures, Titan Mode figures, beast figures and vehicles.
