## Summary

<!--ep:10-->
Jealous of his teammates all being able to achieve Tenkai Titan mode, Toxsa travels to Quarton to train in secret. Meanwhile, Vilius sends out an attack, and Toxsa is captured. The other Tenkai Knights travel to Quarton to save Toxsa. After realizing he needs his friends, He goes Tenkai Titan mode and forces Vilius's forces to retreat.

## Plot

<!--ep:10-->
Feeling jealous of his teammates all being able to achieve Titan Mode, Toxsa decides he needs to train. He first tries to do this on Earth, but then travels to Quarton to train in secret because he is embarrassed.

<!--ep:10-->
Meanwhile, Vilius sends out an attack and Valorn is captured. While held prisoner by Granox and Rho, his mind is scanned for information about the Dragon Cubes, so he instructs the computer to force Granox's device to only take things from his own mind.

<!--ep:17-->
The other Tenkai Knights travel to Quarton to save Toxsa, who manages to escape. Rho then Robofuses into the Flying Darkwing Spector to try and destroy Valorn and the Knights. After realizing he needs his friends, he unlocks Titan Mode and forces Granox to retreat after he destroys Rho with a blast from his staff

## In the episode

<!--ep:10-->
