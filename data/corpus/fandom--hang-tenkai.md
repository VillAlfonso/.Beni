'(Japanese (Katakana): 当の友情) is the thirty-sixth episode of Tenkai Knights. It first aired on May 11, 2014 on Teletoon in Canada.

## Summary

<!--ep:36-->
On Quarton Slygar and Granox have found the tenkai fortress and are arguing about who will take Vilius to it.

## Plot

<!--ep:36-->
Granox and Slyger report back to Vilius on their finding of the Tenkai Fortress. They fight over who was the one that truly found the fortress. Vilius was tired of their fighting, zaps them, and tells them the first one to recover can lead him to the fortress.

<!--ep:36-->
Meanwhile, Guren and the others are in awe over Toxsa's new high tech skateboard(then Ceylan comments about Toxsa actually being like a normal kid) , which lead to Guren wanting one. After hearing Toxsa's story about how he helped out with his family's dinner to get one, Guren helps his father with the local shopping. Mr. Nash, notices Guren is being rather helpful for some reason and thanks him. Guren shakes his head when his father brings up he might be helpful for other reasons then just being nice.

<!--ep:36-->
On their way home, Mr. Nash talks about forgetting to pick up the tiny cat food Max likes and looks around for Guren, only to find him window shopping. Guren tells his father about Toxsa and Mr. Nash makes a deal with Guren, if he does well on his history test, they will talk more about the skateboard. Guren spends most of his free time from then on, studying and learning about ancient history.

<!--ep:36-->
At School, Guren makes a out burst just before the test, to reassure himself that he will ace it. After the test, Guren meets up with the others. Showing off his new skateboard. The boys then all start having fun with the two skateboards, taking turns of racing each other. Ceylan asks Guren if he can ride his board. Guren agrees, saying he trusts Ceylan. While trying to pull off a trick, a dog gets loose and Ceylan tries to put on the breaks, only to fall off the board. The board itself is then send flying out towards the street and is broken in half, which leads to Guren being mad but refusing to say so to the others. In the middle of the group's chat, they get summoned to Quarton.

<!--ep:36-->
Meanwhile, Granox has lead Vilius to the Tenkai Fortress. Vilius talks about how this is indeed the Tenkai Fortress and then asks Granox if he can hold the Fortress. Granox is rather sure of his self, Vilius is not so sure and empowers Granox's beast, turning it into a Ice Rhino from a infusion of dark energy.
Later, Guren, as Bravenwolf, makes a number of rash actions, which lead to things going poorly for him and his allies. Even few of the other Knights point out he might be mad and its fogging his mind. Bravenwolf refuses to say he is mad and rushes forward going Elemental Titan Mode to try and take out Vilius and his minions. Bravenwolf destroys a number of Vilius's minions with a flame slash attack. Vilius shields his self using some sort of a dark energy force field. Bravenwolf keeps trying to destroy it with fire slashes and fails. Vilius then turns his force field into a number of ball shape objects, sending them right at Bravewolf. They hit Bravenwolf and knocks him out of Elemental Titan Mode and sends him falling to the ground. The other Knights try to help Bravenwolf, only to be stopped by Granox attacking them. Bravenwolf is saved by Beag, who talks about how his mind too was once clouded by hate and madness, while flying on a Volt Jet, and how it almost lead to him not making it out of the battle. Beag makes statements how he is great at flying Volt Jets and his passion for flying them. Beag tells Bravenwolf how he can turn a Volt Jet into the Flame Phoenix. Bravenwolf is quick to do so once he learns this. Using the Flame Phoenix, Bravenwolf destroys the Ice Rhino and forces Vilius and the remainer of his army to retreat. Vilius shortly before retreating, talks to Granox about how they almost pushed the Knights to the edge this time and next time will destroy them.

<!--ep:36-->
Later, back on Earth, Guren talks with Ceylan, about how he was mad and how he was sorry. Guren and Ceylan quickly make up. Ceylan holds a brand new high tech scooter and offers the first ride to Guren, only for Toxsa to grab the scooter and give his self the first ride, while Ceylan and Guren are debating.

## In the episode

<!--ep:36-->
- The Ice Rhino and Flame Phoenix make their debut in the show.
- It is revealed that Beag is an expert Volt Jet flyer and he wasn't the original leader of the Corekai. Also, Beag seems to have a passion for flying, since he talks about how much he loves to fly Volt Jets during the episode.
- Granox, even while being on the Ice Rhino when the Flame Phoenix unleashed Supernova, somehow is not destroyed along with the Ice Rhino, which is a theme that has been going on ever since "Vilius Returns", where Granox and Slyger are sent flying as core bricks back to Vilius or are able to take a powerful elemental attack and then force to retreat while in their normal forms.
- It was unrevealed who was the corekai that Beag was jealous of prboraly that corekai got destroyed or killed before season 1 started.
