The Tenkai Knights (Japanese: テンカイナイト, Tenkai Naito, Korean: 텐카이나이트, Tenkai Naiteu) are the protagonists of the series. They are a group of warriors whose sole mission is to protect Quarton from Vilius and his Corrupted army. They are the masters of Tenkai energy. They each have a resemblence to an animal. When they lose all of thier Tenkai energy, the chestplate light goes off.
