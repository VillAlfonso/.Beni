www.tenkaiknights.co.kr is the offical Korean website of Tenkai Knights.

## Media

The website gives information about the anime and the products. It contains info about characters of the first season, Western and Japanese toys, videos, and info about the channels when Tenkai Knights aired in Korea and their schedules for airing new episodes.
