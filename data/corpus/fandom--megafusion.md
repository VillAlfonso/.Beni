Guardian Megafusion (Japanese: ゴーレム, Gōremu, Eng. Translated: Golem, Hebrew: מיזוג מגה) is a mode that only the Guardians have access to use.

## Abilities

Megafusion appears to look like half-transformations of the soldiers' core bricks, built together like legos. Ball joints and parts from Beast Titans are also seen, indicating that the guardians can not only merge the already-present bodies, but generate new ones.

## In the Series

<!--ep:31-->
Guardian Megafusion was first seen in "Dojo Mojo", where Guardian Boreas fused with multiple Corrupted soldiers and went to Core Brick mode. His Megafusion (Japanese: ボレアース・ゴーレム, Boreāsu Gōremu, Eng. Translated: Golem Boreas) represents a giant.

<!--ep:32-->
In "Toxic Toxsa", Guardian Zephyrus fused with many Corrupted soldiers and went into Core Brick Mode. His Megafusion (Japanese: エウロス・ゴーレム, Eurosu Gōremu, Eng. Translated: Golem Eurus) represents a lizard.

<!--ep:33-->
In "Fight Knight", Guardian Notus fused with multiple Corrupted soldiers and went into Core Brick Mode. His Megafusion (Japanese: ゼピユロス・ゴーレム, Zepiyurosu Gōremu, Eng. Translated: Golem Zephyrus) represents a serpent.

<!--ep:35-->
In "Feeling the Heat", Guardian Eurus fused with multiple Corrupted soldiers and went into Core Brick Mode. His Megafusion (Japanese: ノトス・ゴーレム, Notosu Gōremu, Eng. Translated: Golem Notus) represents a falcon.

## Appearance

Boreas is now a golem brick monster with pincers

Zephyrus is now a giant lizard brick monster

Notus is now a giant serpent brick monster

Eurus is now a giant falcon brick monster
