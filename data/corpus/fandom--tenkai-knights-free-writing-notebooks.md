Tenkai Knights Free Writing Notebooks (Japanese: テンカイナイト 自由帳, Tenkai Naito Jiyū-Chō) are free writing notebooks, made by Runa, that were released under the Tenkai Knights product line in Japan in late September 2014 for 324円.
