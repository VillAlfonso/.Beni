13016: Dark Tempest Titan Vilius is a Elemental Titan Figure released under the Tenkai Knights toy line. It was released under the third wave of toys in the western world by Spin Master and in the Korean region by Academy.

Battle against the Tenkai Knights and recreate epic scenes from the Tenkai Knights anime series. Only you can master your Tenkai energy in the fight for the fate of Quarton!
