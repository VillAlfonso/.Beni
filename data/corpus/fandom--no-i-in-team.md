## Summary

<!--ep:29-->
Guren wants to spend time with his dad, but the Tenkai Knights need help. Guren realizes that sometimes work must take precedence over play.

## In the episode

- The Tenkai Fortress is first mentioned in this episode.
- Gen's bike was shown.
