Tenkai Knights Gum (Japanese: テンカイナイトガム, Tenkai Naito Gamu) was an item, made by Top Seika, that was released under the Tenkai Knights product line on July 07, 2014 in Japan for 594円. It included 55 pieces of gum. Characters that you could see on the product were Braven X, Tributon X, Valorn X, Lydendor X, and Kurokishi X.
