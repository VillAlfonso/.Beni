Titan Mode (Japanese: Xモード, X Mōdo, or エックスモード,  Ekkusu Mōdo, Eng. Translated: X Mode, Korean: X 모드, X Modeu, Hebrew: טיטאן מצב) is a powered-up version of a regular Quartonian and can only be activated by certain members of the Corrupted and the Tenkai Knights. To activate it, the user shouts their name and then says "Tenkai Titan Mode activate". It is known that one can receive the power of Titan Mode from another, as Slyger and Granox were given the ability by Vilius. It is currently unknown how Dromus received this power, possibly from either Vilius or Guardian Eurus. This mode and Phoenix Mode was taken away from the Knights by Vilius in "Vilius Returns".

## Appearance

Titan Mode is a larger version of a Tenkai Knight, as it is larger in sheer size. The only difference are add-ons, like the shin guards, armor plating and other accessories.

When activated, the user shifts into brick form and grows into a bigger, more complex brick. The Tenkai Boosters appear and hook themselves on as the legs unfold and the leg armor attaches. The shoulder guards attach as the head and chest plate do the same thing.

## Abilities

Titan Mode gives the user enhanced weapons and powers which cause more damage then when in regular form. The user is also given more strength and has more Tenkai Energy to work with. This form also grants the user limited flight. This is the Knights' most utilized form and their form where they receive the most armor. When two Knights or Corrupted members fuse their powers, they can unlock and use Titan Fusion, a more powerful form.
