Phase 4 Films is a Canadian film distribution company. Its main headquarters is located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It provides service in North America.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

In the U.S. and Canada, Phase 4 Films is responsible for publishing and distributing DVD volumes of Tenkai Knights.
