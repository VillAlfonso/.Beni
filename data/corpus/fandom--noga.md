Noga (fully known as Noga Communications Ltd.) (Hebrew: נגה תקשורת, Nogah Tiḳshoret) is Israel's leading TV broadcast company, located in Herzliya, Israel. The company was founded in September 1996. For more than a decade, the company produces three channels: The Children Channel (Hebrew: ערוץ הילדים, Arutz HaYeladim), Channel 8 (Hebrew: ערוץ 8) (The Documentary Channel), and the Logi Channel (Hebrew: ערוץ לוגי) (The Educational Children’s Channel). Ever since the company was founded, they have been producing over thousands of hours of local programs of every genres and has been buying over a thousand hours of foreign programs from the greatest companies in the world.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

On May 20, 2014, the first episode of Tenkai Knights aired on the Children Channel from 7:30am to 7:50am. New episodes air every week on the Children Channel.
