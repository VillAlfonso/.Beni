Ice Rhinos (Japanese: アイス・ウォー・ビースト, Aisu Wō Bīsuto, Eng. Translated: Ice War Beast) are the ground rumbling mech-beasts of the Corrupted army. Infused with evil Tenkai Energy, these rhino-like beasts rule the ground with the Hos, Grayden, Balthaz, and the War Stallions and attack the Corekai and the Tenkai Knights on command by the evil lord Vilius. They are shown to be a part of Vilius' army and are very loyal. They have been seen to be with Granox.

## Appearance

The Ice Rhino bears little similarity to an actual Rhinoceros and more like the War Stallion with more armor.

## In the Series

<!--ep:36-->
The Ice Rhino made its first appearance in "Hang Tenkai", where Vilius created it from a War Stallion. It was used by Granox, but was later destroyed by the Flame Phoenix.

## Abilities

- Shooting with ice cannons
- Running
- Elemental powers

## Trivia

<!--ep:36-->
- As revealed in "Hang Tenkai", the Ice Rhino is the elemental form of the War Stallion.
