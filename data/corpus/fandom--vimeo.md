Vimeo (Japanese: ヴィメオ, Vuimeo, Korean: 비메오, Bime-o) is a free video-hosting website, founded in November 2004 by Zach Kleinand Jake Lodwick, that allows people to view and comment, share, and upload videos. It's available in English, French, German, and Spanish. Loads of content are available on Vimeo, such as films, episodes of series, shorts, and music videos. Just like YouTube, you don't have to make an account to watch videos, however, the difference is that for anyone who makes an account on Vimeo is able to not get any ads, receive great HD quality on videos, and you gain power with privacy settings.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

Orginally, there were loads of Tenkai Knights episodes available in different dubs. However, one day, all the episodes were taken down.
