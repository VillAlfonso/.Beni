Bravenwolf's Run (Italian: L’impresa di Bravenwolf, Hebrew: מירוץ ברייוונוולף, Spanish: Funcionamiento de Bravenwolf, Dutch: De Race van Bravenwolf, Turkish: Bravenwolf Kaçışı, French: La Course de Bravenwolf, German: Bravenwolf's Lauf) is a Tenkai Knights online game.

## English

Help Bravenwolf fight his way across Quarton. How far can you make it?

## Italian

Aiuta Bravenwolf a combattere e difendere Quarton. Fino a che punto riuscirai ad arrivare?

## Hebrew

מירוץ ברייוונוולף של אבירי טנקאיי - עזרו לברייוונוולף להילחם בדרכו לקווארטון. כמה רחוק תצליחו להגיע?

## Spanish

Ayuda a Bravenwolf en su lucha en Quarton. ¿Hasta dónde llegarás?

## Dutch

Helpen Bravenwolf met zijn gevecht naar Quarton. Hoe ver kun jij komen?

## Turkish

Quarton boyunca Bravenwolf'a yardım et. Ne kadar ilerleyebileceksin?

## French

Aide Bravenwolf à se frayer un chemin à travers Quarton. Jusqu'où arriveras-tu à l'emmener?

## German

Hilf Bravenwolf, sich durch Quarton zu kämpfen. Wie weit schaffst du es?

## How to Play

- Space bar (Key) - Jump/Double Jump
- C (Key) - Dash Attack

## Trivia

- This is the first Tenkai Knights game.
- This game is similar to the endless running game called Robot Unicorn Attack.
