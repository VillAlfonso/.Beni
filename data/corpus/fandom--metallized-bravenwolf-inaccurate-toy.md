## Facts

North America: }}

Inaccurate Metallized Bravenwolf is a promotional Basic Figure released under the Tenkai Knights toy line. This figure was possible to obtain at the NY Toy Fair of 2014. There were some Facebook contests where the Tenkai Knights group would randomly choose a few fans to earn the figure. Inaccurate Metallized Bravenwolf can currently be received by Ebay or by buying the special European version of Tenkai Knights: Brave Battle and is available in the western world and in Europe.
