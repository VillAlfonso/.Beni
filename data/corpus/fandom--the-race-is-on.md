'(Japanese (Katakana): テンカイクエストIV) is the fortieth episode of Tenkai Knights. It first aired on June 15, 2014, on Teletoon in Canada.

## In the episode

<!--ep:40-->
- In the cave, the boys and Beni were given the same weapons they use on Quarton.
- Ceylan reveals that he plays Doom Sploder, just like Toxsa.
- Granox and Slyger's Earth forms and Kiiro's doll, Sandy, make a reappearance.
- This episode introduces five new Robofusion combinations.
- This episode reveals that Venetta can Robofuse.
- More beasts of the Beast World are introduced.
- Ceylan breaks the fourth wall.

## Regional Differences

<!--ep:40-->
- unlike the English version, the Japanese dub shows Granox and Slyger without their suits in Earth form.
