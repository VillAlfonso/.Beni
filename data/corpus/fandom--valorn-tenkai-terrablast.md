Valorn Tenkai Terrablast (Japanese: ガイア・ヴァローン, Gaia Varōn, Eng. Translated: Gaia Valorn, Korean: 가이아 바론, Gaia Baron, Hebrew: ואלורן) is one of the legendary Tenkai Knights. He is owned by Toxsa Dalton, and first appeared in "Tenkai Dragon Cube". His armor animal is a turtle, is the powerhouse of the Tenkai Knights, and has elemental earth powers.

## Original Form

Valorn's color is green, which resembles the outfit of his owner. His armor is based on a turtle. He uses a shield and a spear with the spearhead, which is able to shoot rays. His wings glow green and his eyes glow green.

## Titan Mode

Valorn becomes twice his original size. He has armored legs and arms, as well as a bigger head and a turtle chestplate. He holds a giant Tenkai Energy staff.

## Phoenix Mode

Half of his green parts turn gold

## Elemental Titan Mode

Valorn's Elemental Titan Mode is a bit larger than his original Titan Mode. He has armored legs and arms, as well as a bigger head and a turtle chestplate. He also holds a giant Tenkai Energy staff.
