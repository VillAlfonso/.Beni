The Corekai (Japanese: スペクトロス, Supekutorosu, Eng. Translated: Spectros, Korean: 스펙트로스, Seupegteuloseu, Hebrew: קוריקאי) is an army of soldiers on Quarton that fight alongside the Tenkai Knights. Under the orders from their commander, Beag, the Corekai are battling the Corrupted and trying to defeat Vilius once and for all.
