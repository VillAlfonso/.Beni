Walmart (Japanese: ウォルマート, Uorumāto, Chinese: 沃尔玛, Wò'ērmǎ, Korean: 월마트, Wolmateu) is a multinational enterprise that was founded by Sam Walton on July 2, 1962 in Arkansas, U.S. It is currently owned by the Walton family.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

There are many Walmart stores in the western world that sell the Tenkai Knights toys.
