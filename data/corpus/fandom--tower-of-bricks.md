## Plot

<!--ep:50-->
Dromus is still at Quarton alone while the remaining four Tenkai Knights unwillingly left Quarton. After Beag confirms the same had happened before when the dragons clashed, Gen realizes he really is all alone.

<!--ep:50-->
Vilius claims that now that Dromus is the only one left, there’s nothing stopping him from taking over the Fortress and both worlds (almost certainly because two episodes ago, Dromus fought Vilius and lost).

<!--ep:50-->
Gen decides to go fight again against Vilius, taking the thought of being a Tenkai Knight too with him to give him strength. Villius says that Dromus is stronger since Dromus used to serve him.

<!--ep:50-->
Suddenly, somebody interferes on the fight. It’s Venetta. Once she shows up, Vilius says “Oh look! It’s two Tenkai Betrayers”. Now that Venetta is there too, Vilius says he’s going to punish them for what they did.

<!--ep:50-->
Back to Earth, Guren, Ceylan, Toxsa and Chooki are together while Guren asks why are Granox and Slyger on the tower. Mr. White explains they are there because there’s an anti-brickification machine in the tower and they want to destroy it so brickification spreads all over the world.

<!--ep:50-->
Due to having the machine to protect and leaving Dromus all alone, Guren decides that Toxsa and Chooki are going back to Quarton to help Dromus out and he and Ceylan will protect the machine. However, Toxsa and Chooki can’t make the gate to Quarton work. Becuase of that, Guren decides they should all go to the tower, then.

<!--ep:50-->
While they are still on their way to the tower, Mr. White communicates to Guren that Slyger and Granox have gotten to the top floor of the tower. Right after, Guren tells him that the gate to Quarton didn’t work.

<!--ep:50-->
Then, Toxsa asks Mr. White what the anti-brification machine does and Mr. White gives a brief answer:

<!--ep:50-->
Mr. White also clarifies that he hasn’t been the one building this machine.

<!--ep:50-->
The four main character finally arrive to the tower and Mr. White tells them what to do and also asks for Ceylan to stay on the first floor, leaving Ceylan confused about why him.

<!--ep:50-->
So while he goes to his store, where he somehow has the security commands to the tower (this is shown later on the episode), Mr. White explains to the four main characters that, as Granox and Slyger are there already, the machine isn’t able to keep up with the level of brickification happening at that moment on Earth. The help out the machine, the four Tenkai Knights have to place the four core bricks onto four different places in the tower and that Ceylan’s is on the entrance of the tower. Mr. White also says that what makes this harder is: they need to place the four core bricks at the same time.

<!--ep:50-->
The other 3 go to the elevator. Chooki goes to the 20th floor.

<!--ep:50-->
As Granox looks for the machine and can’t find it and complains about it, Slyger notices there’s somebody else on the tower. So they go to the elevator and attack it when Guren and Toxsa are still inside. Toxsa screams to Guren about using that device that sends the corrupts back to Quarton but Guren says they can’t do it because Chooki is the one who has it.

<!--ep:50-->
The scene changes to Quarton, where Vilius manages to change the good dragon into evil in front of Dromus, Venetta and the Corekai’s eyes. Beag remembers what he saw on the Fortress “if Quarton is in danger, you can summon the great beast” but Beag has no idea how to do it.

<!--ep:50-->
The scene changes back to Guren and Toxsa. They ask Mr. White for help but he has no clue what to do. Then, the elevator gets to the floor where Toxsa was supposed to leave and both leave the elevator then. When the two corrupts try to leave the elevator too, somehow it goes down. Right after, Slyger shows up on the same floor they are at. And Guren and Toxsa find the room where Toxsa has to place his core brick.

<!--ep:50-->
Ceylan comments that all of the Tenkai Knights, besides Guren, have found the rooms to place the core bricks. Ceylan also comments that he hopes Guren is okay. Then, he hears a sound on his floor and Ceylan goes out of the room to ask if it’s Guren who’s out but… It’s actually Granox, but Ceylan is saved because Mr. White just closed two walls between them. Making Granox unable to approach Ceylan. Then Mr. White also finds a way to trap Slyger between walls while he goes after Guren.

<!--ep:50-->
Guren finds the room where he places his core brick and the Tenkai Knights go to the agreement that Guren must be the one giving orders about when they should all place the core bricks at the same time.

<!--ep:50-->
As Guren is about to command, his room starts to shake. Slyger is punching against the door of the room he’s in but Guren decides to ignore and order them to place the core bricks. Mr. White tells Toxsa he put it slightly before the others.

<!--ep:50-->
Slyger makes a hole on Guren’s room door but Guren decides to order them again to place the core bricks into the place and it worked this time. As the Tenkai Energy is felt all over the tower, Slyger and Granox are thrown away from the tower. Granox is just thrown outside because he was on the entrance of the tower, but as Slyger was on the top, he was thrown to the window and then fell.

<!--ep:50-->
After taking out the core bricks, they started to shine, which meant the Tenkai Knights are being called to Quarton. Chooki claims that’s odd because they can’t actually go to Quarton because the gate is not working but Guren says that as they were called to go there now, it may mean the gate can send them to Quarton.

<!--ep:50-->
As they get on Mr. White’s store, Mr White was ready to greet them for the good work but they don’t pay attention because they are just running to get into Quarton.

<!--ep:50-->
Changing the scene to Quarton, one of the Corekai asks Beag for how much longer will the Fortress keep on repairing itself and Beag says he has no idea but he hopes it’ll keep on repairing itself for a very long time.

<!--ep:50-->
Vilius says the Fortress isn’t his biggest priority now. It is to make Dromus and Venetta pay for the betrayal they commited, so he just charges attacks to Dromus and Venetta. However, the four Tenkai Knights show up just in time to save them. After that, Gen explains the others what happened to their dragon.

<!--ep:50-->
Then, Vilius calls the Guardians to fight against the Tenkai Knights. The Guardians attack and the Tenkai Knights can’t do anything against it. As Guren promises the fight won’t end with Vilius winning, Beag says that the fight isn’t over with Vilius winning as long as there’s Corekai still standing, so he challenges Vilius to fight against the Corekai. Vilius accepts and charges his strongest attack to them while Guren yells for them to run away. Then, as the attack is being thrown, Bravenwolf throws himself to the attack to save the Corekai. Villius laughs at Bravenwolf’s stupidity for sacrificing himself to save the Corekai when they are lower than him.

<!--ep:50-->
And after the attack passes through, Bravenwolf is on the floor, not moving. While other Tenkai Knights (Dromus included), Venetta and Beag are around him, crying. They can’t believe Bravonwolf died.

<!--ep:50-->
Then, a part of the Fortress starts to shine, it has the same symbol that is on Bravenwolf. Everybody is confused about what is going on and then… The Tenkai Wolf, the beast from the Fortress, awakes at the same time as Bravenwolf awakes as well.
