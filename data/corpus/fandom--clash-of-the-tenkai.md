Clash of the Tenkai! (Italian: Lo scontro dei Tenkai!, Hebrew: התנגשות הטנקאיי!, Spanish: ¡Choque de Tenkais!, Dutch: Gevecht van de Tenkai!, Turkish: Tenkai Çarpışması!, French: L'affrontement des Tenkai!, German: Kampf der Tenkai!) is the eighth webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins. It was released on November 07, 2013 on Tenkai Knights' official YouTube channel.
