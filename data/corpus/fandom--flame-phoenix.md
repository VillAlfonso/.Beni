The Flame Phoenix (Japanese: ボルケーノ・ジェット, Borukēno Jetto, Eng. Translated: Volcano Jet) is a red and yellow flying vehicle. It's used by the Corekai army.

## Appearance

It resembles a burning phoenix. It is an elemental attack of Bravenwolf's, and it was apparently common knowledge to Beag that Bravenwolf could transform the Volt Jet into it.

## Abilities

- Flight
- Enhanced Speed
- Fire Manipulation
- Supernova Attack

## Trivia

<!--ep:36-->
- As revealed in "Hang Tenkai", the Flame Phoenix is the elemental form of the Volt Jet.
- Before the Flame Phoenix was introduced in the anime, it was orginally named as the Sky Phoenix.

## In the Series

<!--ep:36-->
The Flame Phoenix first appeared in "Hang Tenkai", where Bravenwolf shapeshifted a Volt Jet into it. He combined his elemental power with the Flame Phoenix to use Supernova on Granox and an Ice Rhino.
