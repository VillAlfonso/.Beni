The Tenkai Knights Pen Pouch (Japanese: テンカイナイト ペンポーチ, Tenkai Naito Pen Pōchi) is a pen pouch, made by Runa, that was released under the Tenkai Knights product line in Japan in late September 2014 for 1,296円.
