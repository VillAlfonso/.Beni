Neox (originally called Antena.neox, from 2005 to 2009, and Neox 8, from 2009 to 2010) is a Spanish broadcast channel, owned by Atresmedia, and run by Atresmedia Televisión, that broadcasts children programing during the day and movies and series at night. The channel focuses towards a children and tween audience and is available by satellite, digital terrestrial television (DTT, otherwise known in Spain as TDT), and cable.
