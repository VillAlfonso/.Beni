TK02: Kurokishi X (Japanese: 黒騎士X, Kurokishi X) is a figure, made by Max Factory, that was released under the Japanese Tenkai Knights toy line in Japan on December 23, 2015 for 3,801円.

## Japanese

TVアニメ『テンカイナイト』より、テンカイナイト4勇士のライバル「黒騎士」が最強の戦闘形態、Xモードでプラスチックモデル化! ・接着剤を使用しないスナップフィットモデルです。 ・全身可動で劇中の迫力あるアクションを実現。 ・「ダークネス・ウルフブラック・ソード」「背面ウィング」はクリアパーツで劇中イメージを再現。 ・多色成型と付属シールにより、組立てるだけで設定に近いスタイルとなります。

## English

The mysterious "Dromus" who opposes the Tenkai Knights is joining the PLAMAX series!

From the anime series "Tenkai Knights" comes a plastic model of the Tenkai Knights rival "Dromus" in his ultimate combat form--Titan Mode!
- This plastic model kit makes use of snap-fit connections. Glue is not required for assembly.
- Fully articulated allowing you to recreate action scenes from the series.
- The "Darkness Wolfblack Sword" and back wings are made with translucent parts to preserve their appearance.
- Multiple mold colors and included stickers create a model close to the original style right after assembly.
