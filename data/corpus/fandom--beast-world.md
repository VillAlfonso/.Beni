The Beast World (Japanese: サルラルランド, Sarurarurando, Eng: Sallaland, Korean: 몽키정글랜드, Mongki Jeong-geul Laendeu, Eng. Translated: Monkey Jungle Land) is a parallel place that exists between Quarton and Earth. It is the home of Scorpidon, Orangor, and many other beast-like characters.

## History

<!--ep:38-->
In "Dragon²", when the two opposite Tenkai Dragons attack each other, the Fortress sends everyone, both the Knights, the Corekai and the Corrupted to the Beast World.

<!--ep:39-->
In "Welcome to the Jungle", Orangor and his master decide "to play games" with the new visitors.

## Residents of the Beast World

- Orangor
- Scorpidon (character)
- Gorzorv
- Gorillux
- Scraythe
