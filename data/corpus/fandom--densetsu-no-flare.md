Densetsu no FLARE (伝説のFLARE, Legendary Flare) is the third Japanese ending of Tenkai Knights. It aired from "Vilius Returns" to "Welcome to the Jungle". It was written by Takayuki Yasuda, composed by Shihori, arranged by Naoyuki Osada and performed by Pile.
