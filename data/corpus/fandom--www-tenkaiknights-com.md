www.tenkaiknights.com is the official website of Tenkai Knights. It was launched when Tenkai Knights was officially announced by Shogakukan and Spin Master on June 22, 2013.

## Media

The site features webisodes and also interactive contents such as games. It has a list of their products and step-by-step instructions on how to assemble most of their products. The site is currently available in English, Italian, Hebrew, Spanish, Dutch, Turkish, French, and German.

## Trivia

- Despite that the first season ended back in 2014 for American viewers and that the 2nd season has yet to be officially confirmed as canceled by Cartoon Network or Spin Master, as of 2018, the site is still alive and active for anyone to visit.
