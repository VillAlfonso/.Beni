Victor Entertainment (Japanese: ビクターエンタテインメント, Bikutā Entateinmento), fully called JVC Kenwood Victor Entertainment Corporation (Japanese: 株式会社JVCケンウッド・ビクターエンタテインメント, Kabushiki-gaisha JVC Kenuddo Bikutā Entateinmento), is a Japanese company, founded in 1972, that produces and distributes music, anime, movies, and television shows in Japan. They're a subsidiary of Japan Victor Company.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

Victor Entertainment and Happinet release the Japanese DVD box sets of Tenkai Knights.
