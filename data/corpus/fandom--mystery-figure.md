Mystery Figures (Japanese: ミステリーフィギュア, Misuterī Figyua, Korean: 랜덤피겨, Laendeom Pigyeo, Eng. Translated: Random Figure) are mini figures that can only be obtainable by purchasing mystery packs.

## Appearance

Mystery Figures haven't been seen in the anime. All Mystery Figures have their own core brick, head mold, and a weapon.
