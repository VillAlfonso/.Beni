Target (Japanese: ターゲット, Tāgetto, Chinese: 塔吉特, Tǎjítè, Korean: 타깃, Tagis) is an American retailing company that was founded by George Dayton in 1902 in Minneapolis, Minnesota, U.S.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

Originally, Target stores in the western world was selling the toys of the anime. As of 2015, it was official that Target discontinued selling the Tenkai Knights toys. However, it's still possible to see products on the Target site.
