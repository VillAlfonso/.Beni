TK01: Braven X (Japanese: ブレイヴンＸ, Bureivun X) is a figure, made by Max Factory, that was released under the Japanese Tenkai Knights toy line in Japan on July 30, 2015 for 3,801円.

## Japanese

TVアニメ『テンカイナイト』より、テンカイナイト４勇者のリーダー「ブレイヴン」が最強の戦闘形態、Xモードでプラスチックモデル化！

## English

From the anime series 'Tenkai Knights' comes a plastic model of the leader of the four heroes 'Bravenwolf' in his ultimate combat form - X mode!
- This plastic model kit makes use of snap-fit connections. Glue is not required for assembly.
- Fully articulated allowing you to recreate action scenes from the series.
- The 'Shining Wolf Red Sword' and back wings are made with translucent parts to preserve their appearance.
- Multiple mold colors and included stickers create a model close to the original style right after assembly.
