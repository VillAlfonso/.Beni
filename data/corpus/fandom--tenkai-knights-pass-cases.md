Tenkai Knights Pass Cases (Japanese: テンカイナイトパスケース,  Tenkai Naito Pasu Kēsu, Korean: 텐카이나이트 패스 케이스, Tenkai Naiteu Paeseu Keiseu) are pass cases that were released under the Tenkai Knights product line in Japan in 2015 by Spin Master and ShoPro.
