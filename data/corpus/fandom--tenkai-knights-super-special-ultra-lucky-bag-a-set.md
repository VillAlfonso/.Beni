The Tenkai Knights Super Special Ultra Lucky Bag (A Set) (Japanese: テンカイナイト 超スペシャルウルトラ福袋 Aセット, Tenkai Naito Chō Supesharu Urutora Fukubukuro A Setto) is a special toy bundle released under the Japanese Tenkai Knights toy line. It was released by Happinet in 2015 in Japan for 17,086円.
