X-002: Lydendor X (Japanese: ライデンドールX, Raidendōru X) is a DX Figure released for the Tenkai Knights Japanese toy line. It was released by Happinet on April 5, 2014 in Japan for 1,944円.

## Overview

Lydendor X is anime-accurate unlike its American variant.
