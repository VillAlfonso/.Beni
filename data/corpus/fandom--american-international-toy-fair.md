The American International Toy Fair is an annual event by the Toy Industry Association which sponsors new and future products of new or well known companies to only peers and the press in the Toy Center and the Jacob K. Javits Convention Center of New York City, New York, in the middle of February. The event is not open to the public.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

The 111th annual toy fair, also known as the New York Toy Fair of 2014, NY Toy Fair of 2014, 2014 New York Toy Fair, or the 2014 NY Toy Fair, sponsored toys of many famous companies, like Hasbro, LEGO, Activision, Marvel, and Ionix. Ionix made brick-like toys of How to Train your Dragon 2, Pokémon, and Tenkai Knights. For Tenkai Knights, there were figures of the Knights in Elemental Mode and Elemental Titan Mode, the new look of Vilius in Titan Mode, the Tenkai Dragon in both forms, and vehicles and beasts.
