As revealed on Facebook, the Tenkai Knights Facebook account responded to a fan's question on August 18, 2014, saying that Tenkai Knights (Turkish: Tenkai Şövalyeleri) will air in Turkey within the next 60 days. Finally, it started airing in Turkey on September 01, 2014 at 10:00. Minika GO aired the show everyday at 10:00 until September 26, 2014, where they took a break on new episodes. Sometime later, Tenkai Knights now airs three time a day, one at 11:00, one at 17:00, and one at 20:30.
