Guardians Danger! (Italian: Guardiani in pericolo!, Hebrew: סכנת שומרי העולמות!, Spanish: El peligro de los guardianes, Dutch: Gevaar voor de bewakers!, Turkish: Tehlike!, French: Les Gardiens en danger!, German: Wächter in Gefahr!) is the seventh webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins. It was released on October 30, 2013 on Tenkai Knights' official YouTube channel.

## Plot

Valorn attempts to go through the tower of the Guardians, but instead helps to defeat the Corrupted.
