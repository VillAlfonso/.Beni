The Xenoship (Japanese: ヴィルバット, Virubatto, Eng. Translated: Ville Bat, Korean: 빌 박쥐, Bil Bagjwi, Hebrew: קסנושיפ) is a Robofusion Figure that is formed by toys, made from Spin Master, Happinet, and Academy.

## Description

Robofusion: The incredibly powerful combination of Tenkai Energy that occurs when warriors are in perfect sync. Collect Vilius, Hos, Rho, and Sho to merge their shapeshifting bricks and accessories to create the powerful Robofusion – Xenoship! When the battle gets tough, unlock even more Tenkai energy, shift and combine each character to create Robofusion and soar into battle!

- Revolutionize construction play with the shapeshifting bricks of Tenkai Knights powered by the IONIX construction system!
- Bricks like nothing you’ve seen before. Bricks that come alive. Bricks that shapeshift. Bricks with unexpected surprises. Bricks that unlock your imagination.
- Collect Vilius 10005, Sho 10006, Hos 10007, and Rho (Shadius) 10008 to build the evil Robofusion Xenoship! Downloadable instructions at www.tenkaiknights.com.
- Build your Tenkai power by collecting the entire world of shapeshifting bricks including: humanoid, winged, 4-legged, action, titan, power, and weapon bricks!
