The Corrupted (Japanese: ヴィクリプス, Vikuripusu, Eng. Translated: Veclipse, Korean: 비크립스, Bikeulibseu, Hebrew: המושחתים של) is an army of evil Corekai that are infused with negative Tenkai Energy. Under the orders from their supreme leader, Vilius, the Corrupted searched for the five Dragon Cubes to revive the Tenkai Dragon. Now their goal is to destroy the Corekai and take over the two worlds. They follow Vilius' orders blindly and will battle any army or do any task for him. They are shown to be about one-third of Vilius' army (the rest being made of of mech beasts such as Hos and Sho) and are very loyal. They have many different powers they can achieve after being granted them by Vilius or by being infused with energy from the Tenkai Dragon, such as Robofusion or Titan Mode.
