Ensky (Japanese: エンスカイ, Ensukai), fully addressed as Ensky CO., LTD. (Japanese: 株式会社エンスカイ, Kabushiki-gaisha Ensukai), is a Japanese company, founded in 2005. It's a sub-company of Amada.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

Enksy made the Tenkai Knights Big Sticker Collection.
