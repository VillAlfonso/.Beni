The Guardians Sanctuary (Japanese: ガーディアンサンクチュアリ, Gādian Sankuchuari) is the home of the Guardians. It is home to a Transdimensional Portal, and it is where they conduct all their meetings. It is located on the clouds above Quarton, however it has been found by Vilius and the Tenkai Knights. It was captured by Vilius after the Guardians revived him and was in control of it and the Guardians.

## Under the Guardians' Command

When the Guardians are in control of the Sanctuary, it is a peaceful and serene place, with blue skies and clouds surrounding it. There is a multi-color pad in the middle with one color for each of the Guardians. This pad is where they conduct their voting and holds a hidden portal.

## Under Vilius's Command

When Vilius was in control of the Sanctuary, it was a horrible place with red skies and chaos in the air. He also managed to somewhat reverse gravity, turning the entire place upside down. It was his new command base.
