Tenkai Action Packs (Korean: 미니 액션팩, Mini Aegsyeon Paeg, Eng. Translated: Mini Action Packs), also known as 3-packs, are boxes that contain three Tenkai Knights mini figures with extra pieces and a booklet to show how to build them. These are currently released in the Western world and in Korea.

## Available Packs

For wave one, the available packs are:

- 10501: Bravenwolf/ Hos/ Leinad

- 10502: Tributon/ Vilius/ Kutor

- 10503: Vilius/ Sho/ Lydendor

- 10504: Bravenwolf/ Valorn/ Boreas

For wave two, the available packs are:

- 10505: Bravenwolf/ Shadius/ Deviak
- 10506: Granox/ Notus/ Tavox

- 10507: Tributon/ Vilius/ Senjo
For wave three, the available packs are:
- 10508: Bravenwolf/ Grayden/ Zephyrus
- 10509: Dromus/ Rho/ Tributon
- 10510: Slyger/ Balthaz/ Eurus
- 10511: Bravenwolf/ Senjo/ Deviak

## Trivia

- By buying all 11 3-packs, you could have a total of 525 pieces.
- Wave one=189 pieces
- Wave two=133 pieces
- Wave three=203 pieces
- Bravenwolf is the most occurring character in the 3-packs.
- In the western world, there are 11 3-packs while there's 9 in Korea.
- The Tenkai Action Packs exclusively have 3 Guardians (Eurus, Zepyhrus, and Notus); Ultimately, these 3 Guardians can only be purchased by 3-packs.
- There is exclusivity between the 3-packs.
- 10501, 10510, and 10511 are the only 3-packs to feature at least one 4-legged brick.
- 10503, 10505, and 10511 are the only 3-packs to feature at least one winged brick.
- 10502, 10504, 10506, 10507, and 10508 are the only 3-packs to feature only humanoid bricks.
- Interestingly, there's no 3-pack to have one humanoid, one 4-legged, and one winged brick at once.
