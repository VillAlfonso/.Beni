The Tenkai Knights X Mode Super Modeling Figures (Japanese: テンカイナイトXモード超造形フィギュア, Tenkai Naito X Mōdo Chō Zōkei Figyua, Korean: 텐카이 나이트 X 모드 초 조형 피규어, Tenkai Naiteu X Modeu Cho Johyeong Pigyueo) is a toy line, made by F-Toys, that was released in Japan on February 25, 2015 for 4,104円. The available characters are Braven, Tributon, Valorn, Lydendor, and Kurokishi. They will be released in the Korean region on March 15, 2015.
