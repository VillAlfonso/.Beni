Boreas (Japanese: ガーデイアン・ボレアース, Gādeian Boreāsu, Boreāsu) is a Basic Figure released under the Tenkai Knights Japanese toy line. It was released by Happinet on April 5, 2014 in Japan. It was released in the 2-in-1 Dimensional Dropship/ Portal pack for 5,378円.
