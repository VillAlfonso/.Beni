Mrs. Dalton (Japanese: トクサのママ, Tokusa no mama, Mrs. Kameyama, Korean: 두기 엄마, Dugi eomma, Hebrew: גברת דלתון) is a minor character in Tenkai Knights. She is married to Mr. Dalton, and is the mother of Wakamei and Toxsa. She works at the diner that she and her husband own. She first speaks in "Extreme Titan". She is caring of her children, as seen when she encourages Wakamei when she has doubts about her tough love with Toxsa.

She is voiced by Mary Elizabeth McGlynn in English and by Ai Takanashi in Japanese.
