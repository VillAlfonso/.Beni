Cubification is the process of turning anything that's living and nonliving on Earth into cubes, which was caused by the malfunctioning of the portal at the Guardians Sanctuary. It is similar to brickification. It was first seen in "The Battle Begins". Defeating Vilius and fixing the portal were the solutions to stopping cubification and saving Earth and Quarton.
