Tenkai Knights Frosting Sheets (Japanese: テンカイナイトフロスティングシート, Tenkai Naito Furosutingu Shīto, Korean: 텐카이나이트 설탕을입힘 시트, Tenkai Naiteu Seoltang-eulibhim Siteu) are frosting sheets, which are used for cake decoration, that were released under the Tenkai Knights product line in 2015.
