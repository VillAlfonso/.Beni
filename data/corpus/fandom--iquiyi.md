Iquiyi, stylized as iQuiyi (Chinese: 爱奇艺, Àiqíyì), is a Chinese online video company. It was founded on Aprill 22, 2010 by Baidu. Its headquarters is located in Beijing, China. iQuiyi contains over lots of TV series and movies.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

iQuiyi is the main site to watch Japanese episodes of Tenkai Knights with Japanese and Chinese subtitles.
