## Plot

<!--ep:35-->
The Tenkai Knights are battling against Guardian Boreas. With three of the knights going Elemental Titan Mode, Bravenwolf feels down, for being unable to do the same.

<!--ep:35-->
Later, back on Earth, Mr. White congratulates the Tenkai Knights on a job well done, with everyone but Guren feeling upbeat. Ceylan cracks a joke about Guren being unable to go Elemental Titan Mode, which makes Guren feel even more down. While Guren is thinking of what to do, he travels around town feeling a bit down and unsure about himself, even stopping by the diner to get a drink. Toxsa ends up showing up just after Guren leaves and ends up having to pay for Guren's drink due to him forgetting to pay.

<!--ep:35-->
Then later, Gen shows up and chats with Guren, while Toxsa, Ceylan, and Chooki come up with a plan to take the pressure off of Guren. Gen tells Guren to talk to his team about his lack of Elemental Titan Mode. Whenever Guren tries, the boys try to dodge the topic in order to keep the pressure off of him. They join a beach volleyball game and Gen joins them. Mr. White and Gen both saying in their own way, that maybe Guren is not under enough pressure to unlock Elemental Titan Mode again.

<!--ep:35-->
Meanwhile, Vilius is talking with the Guardians about how they have all failed him. The last guardian the Tenkai knights have not faced yet, Eurus, said the others are too weak to use their full power on the Tenkai Knights. So Eurus was sent off to deal with the Tenkai Knights.

<!--ep:35-->
Then back to our heroes, they get summoned to deal with Eurus attacking. Shortly after the Knights show up, Eurus uses Megafusion to turn into a bird. He starts attacking the Knights, taking them down one by one and Dromus shows up to help save the day, but is also defeated by Eurus. Bravenwolf, on the other hand, is just sitting by helpess, trying to go Elemental Titan Mode, and is only able to do so when Eurus goes in for the final attack. Bravenwolf unlocks Elemental Titan Mode again and is able to defeat Eurus with his elemental power.

<!--ep:35-->
Back on Earth, the boys chat and say their plan to keep the pressure off of Guren worked, only for him to say the pressure helped him to go Elemental Titan Mode. Guren goes to Gen and thanks him for the help, meanwhile on Quarton, Granox and Slyger find what they believe to be entrance to the Tenkai Fortress.

## In the episode

<!--ep:35-->
- Bravenwolf is able to use Elemental Titan Mode again.
- Dromus made a reappearance after so many episodes, however, it seems he didn't unlock Elemental Mode or Elemental Titan Mode.
- This is the fourth episode where Guardian Megafusion appears.
- When Bravenwolf is flying towards Eurus after the first blast of cubes, Bravenwolf wields his sword in his left hand for a brief moment.
- Guardian Eurus was the last guardian to use Guardian Tenkai Megafusion.
- Guardian Eurus was the most corrupted evil Guardian out of the 4 Guardians.

## Errors

<!--ep:35-->
- When Tributon goes into Elemental Titan Mode at the beginning of the episode, he didn't say "Tributon Tenkai Iceblast Titan Mode engage!".
- When Lydendor goes into Elemental Titan Mode, he said "Lydendor Tenkai Lightning Strike engage!".
