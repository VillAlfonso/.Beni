The Tenkai Knights Super Special Ultra Lucky Bag (B Set) (Japanese: テンカイナイト 超スペシャルウルトラ福袋 Bセット, Tenkai Naito Chō Supesharu Urutora Fukubukuro B Setto) is a special toy bundle released under the Japanese Tenkai Knights toy line. It was released by Happinet in 2015 in Japan for 16,935円.
