## Plot

<!--ep:47-->
Vilius is talking to Slyger and Granox claiming that Venetta betrayed him. After ranting about Dromus having found the Black Key he realizes that Venetta betrayed him because she found the Tenkai Stone. Villius swears to get revenge and the stone from her.

<!--ep:47-->
The four boys are wondering about how they can find the stone. Then Guren says it must be like it was with the key: when their core bricks light up once they found the key. So they decide to walk around the city looking for the stone.

<!--ep:47-->
Gen and Beni are talking on the top of the tower, Gen tells her he knows she helped the Tenkai Knights. She changes the subject saying that the view is beautiful, she also adds that she never thought the city was as beautiful as it is, making Gen suspicious. Gen asked what happened to her and Beni says “somebody was nice to me” and shows her pendant with the clover Kiro gave her. Gen is surprised and she then adds “Small gestures can change your perspective”.

<!--ep:47-->
By the end of the day, the boys claim to have looked through the most of the city and haven’t found the stone yet. As it is night and they are tired, Guren tells they should go home.

<!--ep:47-->
Guren arrives home, sees Max and asks if his father is home but Max makes a sad face. Then, Guren sees his father on another division of the house. However, his father isn’t moving. Once he comes closer, he realizes his father has been brickified.

<!--ep:47-->
Even though it’s night, Guren and his friends go to Mr. White’s store to tell about what happened to Guren’s father. Because of this urgent event, they go to Quarton. Due to what happened to his father, Guren attacks Slyger without holding himself back and he easily wins.

<!--ep:47-->
Hoping Guren’s father is okay, they go back to Earth to find Guren’s father still brickified. Guren sits on the sofa, desperate. Meanwhile, Max tries to touch on Guren’s father’s arm but Ceylan catches him on time. However, his father starts bouncing to the front. Toxsa’s notices this and runs to get him and with Ceylan’s help, they both put Guren’s father as he was.

<!--ep:47-->
The next day, the Knights meet again, but Guren didn’t show up.

<!--ep:47-->
The scene changes to Guren, who promises to help his father coming back.

<!--ep:47-->
The scene changes again, this time for Beni, who is picking a new outfit to match the new her but Granox shows up and runs after her. The Knights (without Guren) see Beni running away from Granox and decide to help her because she helped them before.

<!--ep:47-->
Chooki calls Guren telling him that Granox is back and is after Beni and asks for Guren’s help.

<!--ep:47-->
Guren goes to them and they divide into groups, so Beni and Guren are paired together. Then Beni asks Guren if Kiro gave him anything and Guren says she hasn’t. Granox shows up and Beni sends him back to Quarton.

<!--ep:47-->
The Tenkai Knights get called again and Guren goes to Quarton in hope to be able to help his father go back to normal.

<!--ep:47-->
While fighting, the corrupted start running away but Guren forces to go after them so he can have his father put back to normal. So they go to some caves the corrupted ran to. Then they realize it probably is a trap, so they stop. And then they see it really was a trap. Both Valorn and Lydendor get frozen.

<!--ep:47-->
Then Slyger and Granox start bragging about having finally won against the Tenkai Knights.

<!--ep:47-->
Then Venetta shows up and unfreezes Valorn and Lydendor. Thanks to her, the Tenkai Knights can win against Slyger and Granox. Tributon even freezes them but Bravenwolf burns them right after.

<!--ep:47-->
Once the battle is over, Guren goes back home, hoping his father to be okay.

<!--ep:47-->
Meanwhile, Beag finds something odd about the Tenkai Fortress.

<!--ep:47-->
Back to Earth, Guren arrives home and his father is finally okay.
