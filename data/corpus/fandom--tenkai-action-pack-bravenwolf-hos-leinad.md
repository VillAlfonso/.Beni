10501: Bravenwolf/Hos/Leinad is a 3-pack released under the Tenkai Knights toy line. It was released under the first wave of toys in the western world by Spin Master and in Korea by Academy.

Shapeshift and build each character as they prepare for battle. Customize their armor using extra pieces and then unleash their Tenkai weapons!
