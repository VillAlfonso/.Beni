Kmart (Japanese: Kマート, K-Māto, Chinese: 凯马特, Kǎi-Mǎtè, Korean: 케이 마트, Kei-Mateu), stylized as K-Mart, is a American retailing company that was founded in 1962 by S. S. Kresge.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

Just like Target, Kmart stores in the western world were selling the Tenkai Knights toys. When the year became 2015, it became official that Kmart stopped selling the figures. Although, it's still possible to see the figures on the Kmart site.
