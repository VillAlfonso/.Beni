Mr. Black is the alternate version of Mr. White, who only appeared in "Monkey in the City".  His famous quote is "Like a wise man once said...".  He got "destroyed" by the Knights defeating him in the dojo, It was Orangor in disguise.

He is voiced by Brian Beacock in English and by Keiji Fujiwara in Japanese.
