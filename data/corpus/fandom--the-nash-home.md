The Nash Home (Japanese: 大上ホーム, Ōgami Hōmu, Ōgami Home) is the new home, where Guren lives with his father in Benham City. In "Two Worlds", they move into this house. Guren doesn't like the house originally and asks his father: "If I don't like it, can we move back?", who responds "No." But later this attitude changes, as he found a friend already on his first day at school. In the same night, he has a dream in which the Tenkai Knights fight each other. Guren loves his old home, but quickly warms up to it.
