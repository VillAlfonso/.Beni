The Tenkai Dragon (Japanese: テンカイドラゴン, Tenkai Doragon) is a toy released under the Tenkai Knights toy line by Spin Master, Happinet, and Academy.

## Description

Feast your eyes upon the legendary Tenkai Dragon! Known for its unmatched power, both the Tenkai Knights and the Corrupted Army seek to control this mythical winged beast. Battle for control as Bravenwolf and Vilius go head-to-head for the fate of the Tenkai Dragon. Shapeshift this 2-in-1 dragon from good to evil as Corrupted Tenkai energy unleashes the Evil Tenkai Dragon. Loaded with all-new disc launching Action Bricks, The Tenkai Dragon is a deadly and formidable foe. Unleash incredible amounts of Tenkai energy with the unbelievable 2-in-1 Tenkai Dragon! Recreate epic scenes from the Tenkai Knights anime series as you master your Tenkai energy to defeat Vilius and his Corrupted army! IONIX: the revolutionary line of never before seen construction bricks that change their shape before your eyes! These construction bricks shapeshift and change to become mini figures, action figures, weapons, gear, gadgets, and more. What's more, our bricks snap together with other leading construction systems. IONIX: Shapeshift, build, unleash! Tenkai Titan bricks are bigger, badder, and give you more Tenkai energy than ever before! Master your Tenkai Titan energy by shapeshifting in multiple ways to unleash large, ultra-powerful Tenkai figures.

## Features

- Revolutionize construction play with the shapeshifting bricks of Tenkai Knights powered by the IONIX construction system!
- Bricks like nothing you’ve seen before. Bricks that come alive. Bricks that shapeshift. Bricks with unexpected surprises. Bricks that unlock your imagination.
- As the most powerful warrior alive, the 2-in-1 Tenkai Dragon shapeshifts into both a good and evil form.
- Build him up with armor, unleash the deadly disc firing action bricks, and batle head-to-head with Bravenwolf and Vilius mini figures.
