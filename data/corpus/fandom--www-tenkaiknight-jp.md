www.tenkaiknight.jp is an official Japanese website of Tenkai Knights. It was launched a few weeks before Tenkai Knights started its run on the Japanese television station TV Tokyo and its affilates.

## Media

The website informs about the anime and the products. Also it contains a link to the game Tenkai Knights: Brave Battle. Furthermore, it contains comics and information about music and DVDs and events.
