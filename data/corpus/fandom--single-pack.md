Single Packs (Japanese: シングルパック, Shinguru Pakku) are small boxes that contain one Tenkai Knights mini figure, extra pieces for Robofusion, and a booklet for building the mini figure, per pack. These are released in the Western world, Japan, and in the Korean region.
