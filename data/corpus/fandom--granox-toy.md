Granox (Japanese: ブライノックス, Burainokkusu, Brinox) is a toy released under the Tenkai Knights toy line by Spin Master, Happinet, and Academy.

## Western Edition

On the back of Granox's North Amerian box, it says "Granox, amored powerhouse of the Corrupted".

## Description

Granox is the armored powerhouse of Viliu’s Corrupted army willing to devastate theTenkai Knights at the command of his master. Shapeshift from brick to robot and build his armor and shield. Slice into battle unleashing his deadly Tenkai Mace. And when the evil Tenkai energy of one isn’t enough, unlock even more evil Tenkai energy by calling on the combined power of Slyger, Granox, Rho (Shadius), and Sho. Shift and combine each character to create Robofusion and soar into battle as the evil Sickle Ship! Build your Tenkai army and discover an entire world of shapeshifting bricks: humanoid, winged, 4-legged, action, titan, power, and weapon bricks. Each one contains its own unique shapeshifting power and is compatible with leading construction brands. Collect them all to unleash your Tenkai energy. Recreate epic battles from the Tenkai Knights anime series. Only you can master your Tenkai energy to defeat Vilius and his Corrupted army! IONIX: the revolutionary line of never before seen construction bricks that change their shape before your eyes! These construction bricks shapeshift and change to become mini figures, action figures, weapons, gear, gadgets, and more. What's more, our bricks snap together with other leading construction systems. IONIX: Shapeshift, build, unleash!

## Features

- Revolutionize construction play with the shapeshifting bricks of Tenkai Knights powered by the IONIX construction system!
- Compatible with major construction brands, these bricks are like nothing you’ve ever seen before. Bricks that come alive with unexpected surprises.
- Shapeshift Vilius’ corrupted Lieutenant Granox into a poseable figure. Arm him with his shield and Tenkai Mace.
- Collect Slyger 10010, Granox 10012, Rho (Shadius) 10008, and Sho 10006 to build the Robofusion Sickle Ship! Download instructions at www.tenkaiknights.com.
