Televix, fully known as Televix Entertainment, is a company, founded in 1990, that distrubutes documentaries, animation, family entertainment, anime, and feature films. The headquarters is in Beverly Hills, California. They have offices and representatives in San Juan (Puerto Rico), Santiago (Chile), Bogota (Colombia), Sao Paulo (Brazil), and Barcelona (Spain). Televix brings service around the world in countries such as the U.S., Canada, UK, Japan, and more.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

Televix has licensing rights, allowing them to distribute Tenkai Knights in Latin American countries.
