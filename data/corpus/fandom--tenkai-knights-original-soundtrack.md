Tenkai Knights Original Soundtrack (Japanese: テンカイナイト オリジナルサウンドトラック, Tenkai Naito Orijinaru Saundotorakku) is a 2CD audio album, which has a total of 82 songs, made by MONACA. The first CD has 43 songs and the second CD has 39 songs. It will be released in Japan on March 25, 2015 for 3,888 yen.
