In early 2014, Tenkai Knights made its way outside of English-speaking territories. On this occasion, it was also announced to be broadcasted in Italy on K2, which previously broadcasted several notable anime such as Beyblade and Scan2Go. Tenkai Knights (Italian: I cavalieri Tenkai) premiered on June 18, 2014 at 5:25 PM on K2 as a part of the afternoon kids programming. After the airing of "Il ritorno di Beni",  K2 took a break on new episodes until September 24, 2014. Another Italian channel, Quartarete TV, broadcasts the show too.  It is unknown of when Quartarete TV airs new episodes.

## Original

A Benham City nel 2034 quattro adolescenti, Guren, Ceylan, Chooki, e Toxsa, si imbattono accidentalmente in un portale interdimensionale e finiscono nel mondo conosciuto come Quarton, dove vengono scelti dai guardiani di questo misterioso pianeta per diventare la nuova generazione di “CAVALIERI TENKAI”. Su Quarton si trasformano in Bravenwolf, Tributon, Lydendor, e Valorn.
Hanno il destino dei due mondi nelle loro potenti mani robotiche e dovranno padroneggiare incredibili poteri per fermare lo spietato signore della guerra Vilius e il suo esercito di personaggi trasformabili pronti ad attaccare e distruggere sia Quarton che la Terra.

## English (Translated)

In Benham City in 2034 four teenagers: Guren, Ceylan, Chooki, and Toxsa accidentally stumble into an interdimensional portal and end up in the world known as Quarton, which are chosen by the guardians of this mysterious planet to become the new generation of Tenkai Knights. On Quarton become Bravenwolf, Tributon, Lydendor, and Valorn.
Have the fate of both worlds in their powerful robotic hands and will have to master incredible powers to stop the ruthless warlord Vilius and his army of characters convertible ready to attack and destroy both Quarton that Earth

## Trivia

<!--ep:22-->
- Italy is the seventh country to start Tenkai Knights and the second to finish it.
- Furthermore, after France and Belgium, it is the third European country to start Tenkai Knights and the first to finish it.
- Along with Germany, in Italy, Tenkai Knights has the shortest runtime.
- When Italy took a break after the broadcast of "Dromus Betrayed", it was originally thought that the break was going to end on September 05, 2014, but it was pushed to September 24, 2014.
