Minika GO (Turkish: Minika Git) (stylized as minikaGO) is a Turkish broadcaster, owned by the Turkuvaz Media Group, that broadcasts shows by streaming online, satelitte, and cable, towards the age group of 7-14 years old. Their headquarters is at Istanbul. The channel was founded on January 28, 2011 as Minika at Beşiktaş, Turkey. On January 20, 2012, Minika was divided into two channels: Minka GO and Minika ÇOCUK. Minika GO became the replacement for Minika while Minika ÇOCUK received its own channel. There are some notable anime shows on Minika GO like Beyblade, Monsuno, and A.I. Football GGO.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

On September 01 2014, Tenkai Knights was first aired in Turkey. Minika GO airs the show everyday.
