Tenkai Trooper (Japanese: テンカイ トルーパー, Tenkai Torūpā, Korean: 텐카이트루퍼, Tenkai Teulupeo) is a promotional Basic Figure released under the Tenkai Knights toy line. It was released during early 2014, which could have been obtained at movie theaters or Toys R Us in the U.S. This figure can be currently be purchased by Amazon, Ebay, or by buying the first French DVD boxset. It is currently available in the western world, Europe. and in the Korean region.

One Tenkai Trooper Mini Figure.
