Phoenix Titan Mode is a combination of Phoenix Mode and Titan Mode. Only the Knights (excluding Venetta) can use it.

## In the Series

<!--ep:27-->
In "Vilius Returns", Vilius stops the Knights from using Phoenix Titan Mode and places a seal on them, causing them to become unable of using Titan Mode.
