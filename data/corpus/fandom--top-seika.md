Top Seika (Japanese: トップ製菓, Toppu Seika, Eng. Translated: Top Confectionery), fully addressed as Top Seika Co., Ltd. (Japanese: トップ製菓株式会社, Toppu Seika Kabushiki-gaisha, Eng. Translated: Top Confectionery Co., Ltd.), is a Japanese company, founded in 1953, that makes candy.
