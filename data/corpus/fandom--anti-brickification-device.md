The Anti-Brickification Device (Japanese: アンチテトラデバイス, Anchi Tetora Debaisu, Eng. Translated: Anti-Tetra Device) was a machine that reversed the effect of Brickification on Earth. The machine was first seen in "Tower of Bricks". It's located under Benham Tower and was well protected, until Granox and Slyger broke through the barriers. When Slyger broke the machine, the whole planet was brickified, but was reversed when Beag used the Portal Weapon to transport him and Slyger back to Quarton and when the tenkai Knights defeated Vilius.
