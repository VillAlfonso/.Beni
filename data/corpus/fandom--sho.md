The Sho (Japanese: バルタックス, Barutakkusu, Baltax, Korean: 발탁스, Baltakseu, Hebrew: שו) are the flying mech beasts of the Corrupted army. Infused with evil Tenkai Energy, these dragon-like beasts rule the air with the Sky Griffins and Deviak and attack the Corekai and the Tenkai Knights on command by the evil lord Vilius. They are shown to be about one-third of Vilius' army and are very loyal. They have many different powers they can achieve after being granted so by Vilius or by being infused with energy from the Tenkai Dragon, such as Robofusion.

## Trivia

- There is a toy of it, that when combined with Vilius, Rho (Shadius), and Hos, can form the Xenoship, and when combined with Granox, Slyger, and Rho (Shadius), can form the Sickle Ship.
