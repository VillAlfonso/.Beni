Canal J (English: Channel J) is a French children broadcast network, owned by Lagardère Active, that targets the ages from 4 to 16 years old for children. The channel was created on December 23, 1985 at the enterprise of the Hatchette group and launched on the cable network of Cergy-Pontoise. The channel airs well known shows and series like Pokémon, Beyblade, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Scan2Go, and Duel Masters.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

On February 08, 2014, Canal J aired the first and second episode of Tenkai Knights at 4:10pm. One or more new episodes are aired on Saturdays, and sometimes on Sundays, on Canal J.
