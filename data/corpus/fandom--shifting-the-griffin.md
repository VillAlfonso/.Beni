Shifting the Griffin! (Italian: L'avvento del grifone!, Hebrew: משנים את הגריפין!, Spanish: Cambiando de forma a Griffin, Dutch: Hier komt de Griffin!, Turkish: Sky Griffin, French: La transformation du Griffon!, German: Entfesselung des Griffin!) is the fifth webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins. It was first streamed in the United States on October 16, 2013 on Tenkai Knights' official YouTube channel.

## Plot

Villius is about to attack Bravenwolf, when Valorn and Lydendor come in to save him. Then Villius and Bravenwolf engage in battle again, only for Tributon to interrupt with an attack, causing Villius to attack him in on his Volt Jet, and corrupt it. After jumping off the Volt Jet, it became a Sky Griffin due to Villius's power.
