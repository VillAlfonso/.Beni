Valorn (Japanese: ヴァローン, Varōn) is a toy released under the Tenkai Knights toy line by Spin Master, Happinet, and Academy.

## Western Edition

On the back of Valorn's North American box, it says "Valorn, Tenkai Knight of strength, wields the Tenkai Staff.".

## Phoenix Valorn

On the back of Phoenix Valorn's North American box, it says "Phoenix Valorn, powerful phoenix energy supercharges his Tenkai strength!".

Shift and combine each character to create Robofusion and soar into battle as the mighty Air Lancer Jet! Build your Tenkai army and discover an entire world of shapeshifting bricks: humanoid, winged, 4-legged, action, titan, power, and weapon bricks. Each one contains its own unique shapeshifting power and is compatible with leading construction brands. Collect them all to unleash your Tenkai energy. Recreate epic battles from the Tenkai Knights anime series. Only you can master your Tenkai energy to defeat Vilius and his Corrupted army! IONIX: the revolutionary line of never before seen construction bricks that change their shape before your eyes!
