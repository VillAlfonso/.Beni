' (Japanese (Katakana): サルラルランドの大冒険) is the thirty-ninth episode of Tenkai Knights. It first aired on May 30, 2014 on GO! in Australia.

## In the episode

<!--ep:39-->
- The Knights, Vilius and Corrupted henchmen, Beag, and Venetta were transported to a new world, known as Beast World.
- Orangor and a new Corrupted beast are first introduced in this episode.
- The Corrupted Troopers shown in this episode are red instead of black.
- Beag and Orangor's Earth forms are revealed.
- This is the first episode to not contain a scene from Earth nor Quarton.
- Beag learns the true identities of the Tenkai Knights.
- Dromus, Granox, and the Corekai (not including Beag) are seen in this episode.
