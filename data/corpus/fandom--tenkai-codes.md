Tenkai Codes (Japanese: テンカイコード, Tenkai Kōdo, Chinese: 展开代码, Zhǎnkāi Dàimǎ, Korean: 텐카이 코드, Tenkai Kodeu) are dotted picture codes only useable for Tenkai Knights Smart Battle X.

## Ways to obtaining Tenkai Codes

Of course, there are ways to obtaining Tenkai Codes, but only by buying JP merchandise, since Smart Battle X was never released in the western world. Here are the ways.
- Special Edition of Tenkai Knights: Brave Battle
- Tenkai Knights Cube Wars
- Elemental Titan Figures
