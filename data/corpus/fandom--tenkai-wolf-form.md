Tenkai Wolf Form is a mode that only Bravenwolf is able to use.

## Appearance

<!--ep:51-->
This appearance takes place in elemental mode and in gold wolf form. It has enchanced powers and is a very strong good mode. So far, only Bravenwolf can use this mode in Elemental Mode. This mode happens when a Knight mounts the Tenkai Wolf.  Some argue that this mode is Elemental Phoenix mode. This mode first appeared in "Lone Wolf" and also, Bravenwolf got another new sword in this transformation. The transformation turned his shield into another sword making Bravenwolf have 2 swords during the process.

## Abilities

- In some parts where it's red, it's gold
- 2 weapons instead of 1 shield
- Able to fly more faster and more powerful
