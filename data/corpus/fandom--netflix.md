Netflix is an American media-streaming company that was founded by Marc Randolph and Reed Hastings on August 29, 1997. Its headquarters is located in Los Gatos, California. Its streaming services are available to many countries around the world such as Canada, South Korea, and Japan.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

The series is available to stream on Netflix, but there are a few oddities regarding with this. First of all, despite first airing in the U.S. along with Canada, the series can't be played by American viewers for it's not available on Netflix to stream in America. The series can be viewed on Netflix, but only in countries like Spain, Canada, and the U.K. What's even more weird is that Netflix only has the first 26 episodes of the first season, making the other 26 episodes not possible to watch. It's currently unknown if TK will ever be available for American streamers.
