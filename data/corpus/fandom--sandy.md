Sandy (Japanese: サンディ, Sandi) is a small stuffed doll, owned by Kiiro. It was first seen in "Fortress Revealed". Sometimes, Kiiro takes Sandy wherever she goes. She treats Sandy like a living being and a friend.

## Trivia

- Many fans of Tenkai Knights argue that Sandy is a dog or a rabbit.
- It is unknown of how Kiiro obtained Sandy.
