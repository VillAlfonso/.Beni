Tenkai United (Italian: Tenkai Riuniti, Hebrew: טנקאיי מאוחדים, Spanish: Tenkai Unidos, Turkish: Tenkai Birliği, French: Les Tenkai réunis, German: Tenkai Vereint) is the tenth webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins. It was released on November 21, 2013 on Tenkai Knights' official YouTube channel.

- This is the last webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins.
