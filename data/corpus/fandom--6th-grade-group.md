The 6th Grade Group (Japanese: 6年生グループ, 6-Nensei Gurūpu, Korean: 6 학년 그룹, 6 Hagnyeon Geulub) is a group of boys that are in the 6th grade. They are minor characters in Tenkai Knights. They all go to the same school that the Knights (excluding Beni) go to.
