Studio Wanpack is a company involved in Tenkai Knights. They are based in CITY, COUNTRY.

## Tenkai Knights

In May 2013 Studio Wanpack revealed on their website, that they were working on the fifth episode of Tenkai Knights without revealing any other information. One month later it was revealed that Tenkai Knights was a kids series on which Spin Master worked with ShoPro as a follow-up project of Bakugan, to replicate its success with a similar franchise.
