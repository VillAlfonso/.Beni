Dailymotion (Japanese: デイリーモーション, Deirīmōshon, Korean: 데일리모션, Deillimosyeon) is a video-hosting website, founded on March 15, 2005 by Olivier Poitrey and Benjamin Bejbaum, that allows people to share, view and comment, and upload videos. It's known to be one of the largest video platforms around the world and is available in 18 different languages and 35 countries. Its headquarters is located in Paris, France. Apparently, the site was banned and unbanned numerous times in India, but has been banned in Kazakhstan.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

The English Dub episodes of Tenkai Knights used to be on Dailymotion, but they were all taken down. Although, users can, in replacement, watch clips of episodes, commercials, trailers, and toy reviews of the series.
