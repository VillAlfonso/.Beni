Flying Dog (Japanese: フライングドッグ, Furaingu Doggu), stylized as flying DOG, and fully addressed as FlyingDog, Inc., or Ltd. Flying Dog (Japanese: 株式会社フライングドッグ, Kabushiki-gaisha Furaingu Doggu), is a Japanese record company, founded in 1997, that specializes in production animation video and music software production.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

For Tenkai Knights, Flying Dog's job is theme song production.
