Off Balance (Italian: Sul filo del rasoio, Hebrew: יוצאים מאיזון, Spanish: Desequilibrado, Dutch: Uit Balans, Turkish: Dengenin Bozuluşu, French: Le Déséquilibre, German: Aus dem Gleichgewicht) is the second webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins. It was first streamed in the United States on September 24, 2013 on Tenkai Knights' official YouTube channel.

## Plot

The Guardians explain to Vilius that Tenkai Energy must be balanced, otherwise, it will be a disaster for Quarton. They separate the extra energy away from his weapon and give it back to him. Vilius figures out that he can absorb Tenkai Energy and decides to use it so he can weaken his enemies.
