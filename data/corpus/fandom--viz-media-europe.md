Viz Media Europe, fully addressed as VIZ Media Europe, S.A.R.L., is a European company, known for distributing anime and publishing manga. Its headquarters used to be in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, but is now currently located in Paris, France.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

Viz Media Europe is responsible for distributing the anime in countries of Europe.
