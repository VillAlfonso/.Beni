The Tenkai Knights 2015 Calendar (Japanese: 2015カレンダー テンカイナイト, 2015 Karendā Tenkai Naito) is a calendar for 2015 that was released under the product line of Tenkai Knights on October 08, 2014 in Japan for 1,296円. It was created by Spin Master and ShoPro.
