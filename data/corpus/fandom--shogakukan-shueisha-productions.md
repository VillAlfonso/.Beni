Shogakukan-Shueisha Productions(Japanese: 小学館集英社プロダクション, Shougakukan-Shuueisha Purodakushonis), ShoPro for short is a company involved in Tenkai Knights. They are based in Tokyo, Japan.

## Biography

Earlier, they were involved in several notable anime such as Pokémon or Duel Masters. Before Spin Master started collaborating with them, they also produced the spin-off of Spin Master's former smash hit, which was named BakuTech! Bakugan.

## Tenkai Knights

They are a co-producer of Tenkai Knights, along with Spin Master. On June 22, 2013, it was announced that Shogakukan and Spin Master worked on Tenkai Knights, which was announced to premiere two months later. In February 2014, it also became known that Tenkai Knights was finally brought to Japan. As Tenkai Knights is mainly a Japanese production, only with partly Canadian funding and little creative input, Shogakukan probably has more influence on the production.

## Trivia

- Shogakukan is known in the anime fanbase for being very strict when dealing with illegal uploads. In particular on YouTube where they upload anime episodes themselves (only for Japan), they often take down videos which even only have little content taken from them. Although English episodes were uploaded by Spin Master, Shogakukan took them down, as well as later the Japanese opening of Tenkai Knights, which was after that uploaded on Vimeo.
