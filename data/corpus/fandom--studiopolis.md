Studiopolis, fully addressed as Studiopolis Inc., is a American production company that specializes in dubbing and voiceover recording for video games, anime, and cartoons. They are located in Studio City, Los Angeles, California. Before 2005, Studiopolis originally used to be known as Screenmusic Studios and then Studio E Productions.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

Studiopolis is accountable for producing the English dub of Tenkai Knights.
