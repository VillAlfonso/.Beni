Gmarket (Japanese: ジーマーケット, Jīmāketto, Korean: G마켓, G-Makes), stylized as G-Market, is a Korean online retailing company that was founded in 1999 by Young Bae Ku.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

Products from the Korean Tenkai Knights line can be purchased from Gmarket.
