Friends or Foes? is a paperback by Brandon T Snider. It will be published on June 09, 2015.

## Official Description

Can these new Knights be trusted? Gen and Beni are new to Benham City, and Guren and the gang do their best to give them a good welcome. But these two newcomers seem to have plans of their own--on Earth, as well as Quarton. The boys must figure out what these strangers want before it's too late!
