Pick Your Path: #1 Defenders of Quarton is a paperback by Ray Santos. It was published on September 25, 2014. IGN rated this book 8.5/10, and called it "engaging" and "enjoyable enough".

## Overview

In Tenkai Knights: Defenders of Quarton, your choices can affect the outcome of the story. Some of the choices are very hard to make and others are obvious in my opinion. However you don't lose in any of the endings some are just better than others.

## Official Description

Find your way through the world of Tenkai Knights!

This pick-your-path chapter book will have readers guessing at every turn! Complete with black-and-white line art, readers can pick their way through the Tenkai Knights story to create their own unique tale.
