Guardian Zephyrus (Japanese: ガーデイアユーラス, Gādeian Yūrasu, Eng. Translated: Guardian Eurus, Korean: 가디언 에우로스, Gadieon Euroseu, Hebrew: אוּרוּס) is one of the four Guardians of the portal to Quarton and Earth in Mr. White's shop and the Guardians Sanctuary, as shown in "Tenkai Knights: Origins".

He is voiced by Matthew Mercer in English and by Michiko Neya in Japanese.

## Original Form

- Flight
- Deflection
- Enhanced Speed
- Proficient Marksman
- Great wielder of Tenkai Energy

## Black Guardian Form

- Flight
- Deflection
- Enhanced Speed
- Proficient Marksman
- Great wielder of Tenkai Energy

## Trivia

- He was the third Guardian to have his name revealed.
- In the Japanese version of the show, Zephyrus' name is Eurus.
- Zephyrus' name and color are based off of the Greek god of the west wind. Zephyrus (Greek: Ζέφυρος, Zéphyros), in Greek mythology, was a symbol for the season of spring, which best represents the color, green.
