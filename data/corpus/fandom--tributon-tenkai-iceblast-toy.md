Tributon Tenkai Iceblast (Japanese: ブリザード・トリビュトン, Burizādo Toribyuton, Eng. Translated: Blizzard Tributon) is a toy released under the Tenkai Knights toy line by Spin Master, Happinet, and Academy.

## Japanese Edition

Tributon has two arrows and has two capes. His helmet ear section is colored light blue while the middle helmet is dark blue. His body is colored half light blue and half dark blue just like the series.
