The Core Corrupter (Japanese: 反転装置, Hanten Sōchi, Eng. Translated: Inversion Equipment, or Anti-Rotating Device, Korean: 반전 장치, Banjeon Jangchi) is a device that Dromus developed in the episodes before "Two Sides to Every Coin", where it was finally used. It transforms the Corekai's vehicles into the Corrupted's minions. They turn from vehicle to animal, vehicle brick to beast brick. There are two known forms of this: BlasTank to War Stallion and Volt Jet to Sky Griffin. This process, however can be reversed by destroying the corrupter part that attaches to the Vehicle/Animal.
