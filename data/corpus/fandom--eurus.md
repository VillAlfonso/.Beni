Guardian Eurus (Japanese: ガーデイアン・ノトス, Gādeian Notosu, Eng. Translated: Guardian Notus, Korean: 가디언 노토스, Gadieon Notoseu, Hebrew: נוֹטוּס) is one of the four Guardians of the portal to Quarton and Earth in Mr. White's shop and the Guardians Sanctuary, as shown in "Tenkai Knights: Origins".

He is voiced by Kyle Hebert in English and by Hideyuki Umezu in Japanese.

## Trivia

<!--ep:25-->
- Eurus was the second Guardian to have his name revelaed.
- In "The Dark Unlock", its been revealed that he gave Gen the core brick of Dromus.
- Vilius revealed that it was Eurus's Idea to awaken the evil Dragon.
- In the Japanese version of the show, Eurus' name is Notus.
- Eurus' name and color are based off of the Greek god of the east wind. Eurus (Greek: Εὖρος, Euros), in Greek mythology, was a symbol for rain, warmth, and unluckiness, which best represents the color, red.
- His Guardian megafusion represents a Falcon.
- He and Trubiton have never fought in the show.

## History

<!--ep:27-->
Eurus was first seen in "The Key to Evil" when he apparently "assisted" the Tenkai Knights in obtaining a new power. He has apparently been assisting Beni and Gen in their efforts to find the Black Key. He claims to have done these things because he believes Boreas has upset the balance in Tenkai Energy, which he wishes to correct. In "A New Knight", he claims to want to help the other Guardians after Vilius went too far. He was captured by Viliius in "Vilius Returns" and was a part of Vilius' scheme.
