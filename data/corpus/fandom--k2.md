K2 is an Italian children's television show broadcaster owned by Discovery Networks Europe. It primarily airs shows for children from ages of 4 to 14 and sometimes airs shows garnered for people above the age of 15. K2 used to be a block in 2004 but it became a TV channel in 2009 as it got bought by Switchover Media. But starting in 2013 it got bought by Discovery Networks Europe even when K2 rebranded on June 30th of 2013, and as of 2009 to now is available by a digital terrestrial network. The channel features famous series and shows like Total Drama, Pokémon (Since DP: Battle Dimension and they air more seasons & the recent ones), Beylade, Yu-Gi-Oh! (Zexal, Arc-V & Vrains), Scan2Go, and Kirby: Right Back at Ya!.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

On June 18, 2014, the first episode of Tenkai Knights aired at 5:25pm (Italy Timezone) on K2. Italy's pattern of airing new episodes is interesting. One episode airs per day, but eventually comes to a point where K2 takes a break for three days, and then broadcasts new episodes one a day again.
