The Haksan Culture Company (Korean: 학산문화사, Haksan Munhwa-Sa), also called Haksan Cultural History, is a South Korean company, founded in 1995, specialized in publishing comics and light novels.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

The Haksan Culture Company made some books for Tenkai Knights.
