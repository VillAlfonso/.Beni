Ultra Guardian Megafusion is a mode that only Vilius has access to using.

## Appearence

This appearance consists of more than 100, and I mean a thousand bricks. Only Vilius can use this mode with the evil guardians. This form is the most powerful mode Vilius has ever used against the Tenkai Knights.

## Abilities

- Long arms and legs
- Enhanced Strength
