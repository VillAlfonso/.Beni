Senjo (Japanese: スペクトロス・イエローウォーリアー, Supekutorosu Lerō Wōriā, Spectros Yellow Warrior, Korean: 옐로워리어, Yello Worieo, Hebrew: סנג'ו) is a gray and yellow Tenkai Trooper in the Corekai army.
