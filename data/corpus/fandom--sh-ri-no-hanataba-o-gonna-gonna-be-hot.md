Shōri no Hanataba o -gonna gonna be hot!- (勝利の花束を -gonna gonna be hot!-, A Bouquet of Victory -gonna gonna be hot!-) is the second Japanese ending theme of Tenkai Knights. It aired from "Lost Key" to "A New Knight". It was written by SAKI, composed by AZU, arranged by Ryosuke "Dr.R" Sakai & Cyntia and sung by Cyntia.
