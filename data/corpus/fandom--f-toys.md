F-Toys (Japanese: エフトイズ, Efutoizu), fully addressed as F-Toys Confect Co., or Ltd. F-Toys Confect (Japanese: 株式会社エフトイズ・コンフェクト, Efutoizu Konfekuto), is a Japanese company, founded in 2001, that's known for making plastic models of airplanes, tanks, battleships, and starships.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

F-Toys made Metal Tags and X Mode Hyper Figuration Figures.
