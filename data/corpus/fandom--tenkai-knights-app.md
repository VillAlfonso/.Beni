Tenkai Knights is an app that was created by iStoryTime, Inc. on January 23, 2014. It's latest version is Version 1.11.

## Official Info

Join the Tenkai Knights, masters of shapeshifting Tenkai Energy and Robofusion, in their fight to save the world as they use their amazing powers to stop the evil Vilius and his army of shapeshifting robots from destroying Quarton.

FEATURES:
Includes animation of each character brick shapeshifting into Tenkai Knights
- Includes real images from the show for an authentic experience
- Offers automatic navigation
- Includes three narration options for ease of use: read to me, auto play and read to myself

ADDITIONAL STORY INFORMATION:
Saving the world is serious business and should never be taken lightly... It is only for the brave of heart. Guren and Ceylan were not planning on being heroes when they accidentally stumbled into an inter-dimensional portal. Ripped through space and time, they reemerged on the world of Quarton as Bravenwolf and Tributon … the legendary and powerfully robotic Tenkai Knights! Oh, by the way, Quarton is definitely a world like no other.
