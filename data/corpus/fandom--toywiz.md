ToyWiz (Japanese: おもちゃウィズ, Omocha Uizu, Chinese: 玩具'奇才, Wánjù Qícái, Korean: 장난감 비상한솜씨, Jangnangam Bisanghansomssi) is an American retailing company that was founded over 20 years ago in Nanuet, NY, U.S. Its website was founded on February 20, 1999.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

Tenkai Knights toys can be bought on ToyWiz.
