Kurokishi (Japanese: 黒騎士, Kurokishi) is a Basic Figure released under the Tenkai Knights Japanese toy line. It was released by Happinet on April 5, 2014 in Japan. It was included with Spectros Red Warrior in the 2-in-1 BlasTank/ War Stallion pack for 4,298円 and was included with Braven in Tenkai Knights Cube Wars, which was released by Happinet on July 12, 2014 in Japan for 4,298円.
