Bravenwolf Tenkai Firestorm (Japanese: バーニング・ブレイヴン, Bāningu Bureivun, Eng. Translated: Burning Braven) is a toy released under the Tenkai Knights toy line by Spin Master, Happinet, and Academy.

## Japanese Edition

Bravenwolf Tenkai Firestorm has 2 capes side ways. His head is bigger, more anime accurate and has flames on top. He now has two swords in this toy form.
