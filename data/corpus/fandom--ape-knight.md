## In the episode

<!--ep:43-->
- Guren was turned into an ape for a temporary amount of time.
- In the third round of the robofusion race, the Knights won, but never got their wish.
- More new beasts are introduced in the jungle.
- It is also revealed that Toxsa can only think about food and is not careful of where he gets it from.
- Toxsa reveals in the episode that he is gonna miss the chocolate covered French fries which might mean they are served in the turtle diner.
- The knights give up complaining and duel Scorpidon.
- Orangor gives all the knights except for Guren a task, and the hint is something that Beni doesn't understand.

- Guren's monkey form saves Ceylan from being caught from the monkeys.
