Mr. Inukai (Japanese: ゲンのパパ, Gen no papa, Korean: 겐 아빠, Gen appa) is a minor character in Tenkai Knights. He is Gen's father. It is currently unknown of what happened to him. He either passed away or is located somewhere else on Earth.

He is voiced by Tomohiro Tsuboi in Japanese.

## In the Series

<!--ep:18-->
Mr. Inukai was first seen in a flashback in "The Key to Evil", where he said something that caused Gen to be sad, forcing him to drop his basketball.

<!--ep:25-->
In "The Dark Unlock", in another flashback, he and Gen are in an apartment room or house. He is crying about it the fact that he lost his job and his wife divorced him. He asks Gen if he still has faith in him. The two then hug each other and Mr. Inukai cries loudly.

## Trivia

- Mr. Inukai is one of the few characters to not have a voice acting role in the English version.
- Mr. Inukai has a larger role in the Japanese version than he does in the English version.
