The Tenkai Knights Carry Case (Japanese: テンカイナイト・キャリーケース, Tenkai Naito Kyarī Kēsu, Korean: 텐카이 나이트 휴대용 케이스, Tenkai Naiteu Hyudaeyong Keiseu) is a case released for the Tenkai Knights Japanese toy line. It was released by Happinet on April 5, 2014 in Japan for 1,058円. It can hold Basic Figures and DX Figures.
