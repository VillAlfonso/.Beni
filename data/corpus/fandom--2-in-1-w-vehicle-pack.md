## Facts

North America: }}

2-in-1/ W Vehicle Packs are large boxes that contain 3 parts: a core brick, many pieces to build two different figures that share the same brick, and two booklets that show how to build the two individual figures. These are released in the Western world, known as 2-in-1 packs, and in Japan, known as W Vehicle packs.
