Hos (Japanese: トロックス, Torokkusu, Taurox, or Torox) is a toy released under the Tenkai Knights toy line by Spin Master, Happinet, and Academy.

## Western Edition

On the back of Hos' North American box, it says "Hos, his evil bite is worse than his bark.".

## Description

Hos, the shapeshifting bull-like mech-beasts of the Corrupted army plague the Tenkai Knights at every turn. Shapeshift from brick to 4-legged Quadrax Trooper and build up his armor. Charge into battle unleashing his deadly Tenkai energy weapon. And when the evil Tenkai energy of one isn’t enough, unlock even more Tenkai energy by calling on the combined power of Hos, Sho, Rho (Shadius), and Vilius. Shift and combine each character to create Robofusion and soar into battle as the evil Xenoship! Build your Tenkai army and discover an entire world of shapeshifting bricks: humanoid, winged, 4-legged, action, titan, power, and weapon bricks. Each one contains its own unique shapeshifting power and is compatible with leading construction brands. Collect them all to unleash your Tenkai energy. Recreate epic battles from the Tenkai Knights anime series. Only you can master your Tenkai energy to defeat Vilius and his Corrupted army! IONIX – the revolutionary line of never before seen construction bricks that change their shape before your eyes! These construction bricks shapeshift and change to become mini figures, action figures, weapons, gear, gadgets, and more. What's more, our bricks snap together with other leading construction systems. IONIX – Shapeshift, build, unleash!
