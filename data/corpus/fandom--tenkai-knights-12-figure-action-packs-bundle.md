The Tenkai Knights 12 Figure Action Packs Bundle is a special toy bundle released under the Tenkai Knights toy line. It was released online, specifically on Amazon, by Spin Master, in 2015 for $44.99.

## Official Description

Bundle includes Ionix Tenkai Knights - Action Pack 10504 (Valorn/Bravenwolf/Guardian) Ionix Tenkai Knights - Action Pack 10503 (Vilius/Sho/Lydendor) Ionix Tenkai Knights - Action Pack 10501 (Bravenwolf/Leinad/Hos) Ionix Tenkai Knights - Action Pack 10502 (Vilius/Kutor/Tributon)
