'(Japanese (Katakana): ねらわれたセイラン) is the thirtieth episode of Tenkai Knights. It first aired on March 30, 2014 on Teletoon in Canada.

## Summary

<!--ep:30-->
Vilius sends Granox to Earth in order to get revenge on Tributon.

## Background

<!--ep:38-->
- This episode aired in the U.S. on the day of the Japanese premiere of Tenkai Knights.
- This episode re-aired on September 5, 2014 in the U.S., the day before new episodes were broadcasted again on Cartoon Network, starting with "Dragon²".

## In the episode

<!--ep:30-->
- Granox's Earth form and Ceylan's mother are revealed in this episode.
- Tributon was the fourth Knight to use his elemental attack.
- Toxsa says "All your base are belong to us!", which is a popular internet meme.
