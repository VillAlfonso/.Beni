'(Japanese (Katakana): ブラックガーディアン誕生！) is the thirty-first episode of Tenkai Knights. It first aired on April 06, 2014 on Teletoon in Canada.

## Summary

<!--ep:31-->
After the Guardians were brainwashed by Vilius' black Dragon Cubes, the Tenkai Knights must learn how to defeat them.

## In the episode

<!--ep:31-->
- Elemental Titan Mode is introduced by Bravenwolf being the first to use this ability.
- This episode features Gen's first major appearance since "A New Knight".
- This episode reveals that Gen attends a Dojo.
- This episode features the Knights's first training with Mr. White since they unlocked Titan Mode in "The Tenkai Kid".
- This episode reveals that Gen is a 1st Degree Black Belt Master.
- This episode reveals that Mr. White is a 3rd Degree Black Belt Ninja Master.
- This is the first episode in which Guardian Megafusion appears.
- When first giving out the rice bowls, Gen mistakenly gives Guren and Ceylan their opposite colors, blue and red, respectively.
- Boreas is the first guardian to do guardian tenkai Meagfusion.
- The guardian's declined to help Vilius , But Vilius corrupted them with 4 black dragon cubes making them evil and the ability to do guardian tenkai megafusion.
