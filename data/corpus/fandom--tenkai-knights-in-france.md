In early 2014, Tenkai Knights (French: Les chevaliers Tenkai) was announced to air in France. Canal J, a profiled children's broadcaster, was charged with the broadcast of Tenkai Knights there. On February 08, 2014 at 4:10 PM, the first two episodes were broadcasted. New episodes were aired every week until April 27, 2014. Tenkai Knights returned on Canal J in September 2014 and is airing new episodes again. Another broadcaster, Gulli, aired the first two episodes of Tenkai Knights on April 12, 2014 at 9:15 AM. However, no more new episodes aired after that. It was revealed that on September 03, 2014, new episodes were broadcasted again. Currently, Tenkai Knights airs on Gulli at 10:40.

## Original

Quatre adolescents, Guren, Ceylan, Chooki, et Toxsa, sont accidentellement transportés à travers un portail inter-dimensionnel. Ils atterrissent dans un monde connu sous le nom de Quarton. Dans ce monde, ils deviennent Bravenwolf, Tributon, Lydendor, et Valorn: les 4 légendaires chevaliers Tenkai.

Désormais, nos quatre amis détiennent le destin de deux mondes entre leurs mains. Ils devront maîtriser leurs immenses pouvoirs pour empêcher le démoniaque Vilius et son armée de robots de détruire la planète Quarton ainsi que la Terre!

Ils n’avaient pas prévu de devenir des héros et ne sont pas au bout de leurs surprises…
