TV Setouchi (Japanese: テレビせとうち, Terebi Setōchi) is a Japanese TV channel, founded in 1984. It is owned by Sanyo Shimbun, Nikkei, Kabaya, and the Prefectural Government of Okayama.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

TV Setouchi aired the Japanese version of Tenkai Knights.
