Tenkai Knights Square Can Cases (Japanese: テンカイナイトスクエア缶ケース, Tenkai Naito Sukuea Kan Kēsu) are tin cases, made by Banpresto, that were released under the Tenkai Knights product line in Japan on September 23, 2014 for 150円.
