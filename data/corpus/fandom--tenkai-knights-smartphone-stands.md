Tenkai Knights Smartphone Stands (Japanese: テンカイナイトスマートフォンスタンド,  Tenkai Naito Sumātofon Sutando, Korean: 텐카이나이트 스마트폰 스탠드, Tenkai Naiteu Seumateupon Seutaendeu) are phone stands that were released under the Tenkai Knights product line in Japan in 2015 by Spin Master and ShoPro.
