## Summary

<!--ep:37-->
Inside the cave the Knights discover the tenkai fortress behind a barrier that they are unable to penetrate.

## In the episode

<!--ep:37-->
- This is the first episode where one of Chooki's relatives are shown, which in this case is his little cousin.
- The Fire Raptor and the Ice Walker make their debut in the show.
- What the Tenkai Fortress looks like is revealed.
- Kiiro makes her first appearance and brings a stuffed dog animal named Sandy.
