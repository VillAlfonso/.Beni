The Tenkai (Japanese: テンカイ, Tenkai, Korean: 텐카이, Tenkai) are a species of shapeshifting robots, disguised as toy bricks (known as Lego), from the planet Quarton. This species is known for its ability to generate Tenkai Energy, the most valuable source in the universe. The Tenkai are also known as Quartonians.
