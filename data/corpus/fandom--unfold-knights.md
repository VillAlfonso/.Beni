Unfold Knights (Japanese: くりひろげるナイト, Kurihirogeru Naito, Chinese: 展開騎士, Zhǎnkāi Qíshì, or 展开骑士, Zhǎnkāi Qíshì, Korean: 접혀있는 기사, Jeobhyeoissneun Gisa) is a toy line, made by LeLe, that was released under the Tenkai Knights toy line in the Korean region in late 2014.
