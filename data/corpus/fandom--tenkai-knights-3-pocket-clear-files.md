Tenkai Knights 3-Pocket Clear Files (Japanese: テンカイナイト 3ポケットクリアファイル, Tenkai Naito 3 Poketto Kuria Fairu) are 3-pocket clear files, made by Runa, that were released under the Tenkai Knights product line in Japan in late September 2014 for 432円.
