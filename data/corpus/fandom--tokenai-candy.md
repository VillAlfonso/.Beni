Tokenai Candy (溶けないCANDY, Unmelting Candy) is the fourth and final ending of Tenkai Knights. It was played from "The Race is On" to "Lone Wolf". The song was written by Natsumi Tadano, composed by Kōtarō Egami and Justin Moretz, arranged by Egami, Moretz and Takanori Tsunoda and sung by Gacharic Spin.
