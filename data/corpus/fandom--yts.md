YTS, fully known as Yamagata Television System (Japanese: 山形テレビシステム, Yamagata Terebi Shisutemu), is a Japanese broadcasting company, founded in 1970.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

One of the channels YTS owns is Yamagata TV, which aired the Japanese version of Tenkai Knights.
