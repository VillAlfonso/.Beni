The Chicago Toy & Game Fair, also known as the ChiTAG Fair, was an annual event by the Chicago Toy & Game Group which sponsors toys and games from well-known companies around the world to the press, bloggers, and the public at Navy Pier, Chicago, on November 23 and 24, 2013.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

The 11th annual Chicago Toy & Game Fair, also called the ChiTAG Fair of 2013, 2013 ChiTAG Fair, and the 11th annual ChiTAG Fair, featured toys and games from companies like Hasbro, Razor, and Spin Master. For Spin Master, there was plenty of Tenkai Knights figures. Play tables, clips, and demos were set for the show at a booth. A contest called the Ionix Shapeshift Challenge was held for children and builders to test their construction skills.

The 12th ChiTAG Fair will be held this November. Its unknown if anything of Tenkai Knights will be at the ChiTAG fair again.

## Trivia

- This is the only annual event in the U.S. to allow public access.
