## How to Play

- Arrow Keys - Move
- Space bar (Key) - Shoot
- Z (Key) - Phoenix Attack
- X (Key) - Homing Laser
A yellow core brick gives you energy, a blue core brick gives you missiles, and a red core brick gives you more health.
