AV Visionen (English: AV Visions), fully called AV Visionen GmbH, is a media company, founded in 2002, that specializes in producing DVDs, comics, and manga of anime. They're a subsidiary of Viz Media Europe. They bring service in the GAS countries (Germany, Austria, and (German-speaking) Switzerland) of Europe.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

AV Visionen makes the German DVD volumes of Tenkai Knights.
