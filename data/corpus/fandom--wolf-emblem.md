The Wolf Emblem (Japanese: ウルフの紋章, Urufu no Monshō) is a gift that Orangor gave to Bravenwolf when he and the other Knights, Beag, and Vilius and some of his troopers returned to Quarton. When the Wolf Emblem merges with the Tenkai Fortress, the Tenkai Wolf would be awakened.

## In the Series

<!--ep:43-->
In "Scorpidon", the Knights, Beag, and Vilius and some Corrupted troopers were transported from the Beast World back to Quarton. The Knights and Vilius, while in Elemental Titan Mode, were back in the cave that had the Tenkai Fortress. Orangor decides to give Bravenwolf the Wolf Emblem and then teleports back to the Beast World.
