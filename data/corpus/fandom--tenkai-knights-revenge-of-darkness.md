Tenkai Knights: Revenge of Darkness (Japanese: テンカイナイト闇の逆襲, Tenkai Naito: Yami no Gyakushū, Korean: 텐카이나이트 어둠의 역습, Tenkai Naiteu: Eodum-ui Yeogseub, Eng. Translated: Tenkai Knights: Revenge of the Dark) is a Korean toy-animated series that was created in August 2015 by Academy.

## General Information

Tenkai Knights: Revenge of Darkness consists of episodes (15 seconds long) that uses the toys from the toy line.
