The Tenkai Knights Big Sticker Collection (Japanese: テンカイナイト ビッグステッカーコレクション, Tenkai Naito Biggu Sutekkā Korekushon) is a sticker container box, made by Ensky, that was released under the Tenkai Knights product line in Japan in late August 2014 for 2,160円.
