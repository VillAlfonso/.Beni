The Volt Jet (Japanese: ボルト・ジェット, Boruto Jetto, Eng. Translated: Bolt Jet, Korean: 볼트 제트, Bolteu Jeteu) is a black and blue flying vehicle. It's used by the Corekai army.

## Trivia

<!--ep:36-->
- In "Hang Tenkai", the elemental form of the Volt Jet is the Flame Phoenix.

## Notes

There is a toy of it that can be turned into a Sky Griffin, which includes Tributon and Pivix.

## In the Series

<!--ep:11-->
Volt Jets are first seen in "Two Sides to Every Coin", where they were shooting the Corrupted soldiers. When Dromus used the Core Corrupter on them, they were turned the into Sky Griffins, but were later revived by the Tenkai Knights to serve again for the Corekai.
