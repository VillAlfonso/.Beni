Kutor (Japanese: スペクトロス・ブルーウォ―リア―, Supekutorosu Burū Woriā, Spectros Blue Warrior, Korean: 블루워리어, Beullu Worieo, Hebrew: קוטור) is a white and blue Tenkai Trooper in the Corekai army.

## Trivia

<!--ep:17-->
- There is also a toy of him, that when combined with Leinad, Tributon, and Bravenwolf can form the Protojet.
- Most blue Corekai soldiers seem to be named Kutor, however, few are ever adressed.
- Blue kutors are loyal to the Tenkai knights but closest to Tributon., As shared the same color.
