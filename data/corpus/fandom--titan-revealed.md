Titan Revealed (Italian: La Rivelazione Titan, Hebrew: חשיפת טיטאן, Spanish: Titán Descubierto, Dutch: Titan onthuld, Turkish: Titanlar, French: Le Titan se revèle, German: Entdeckung des Titan) is the ninth webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins. It was released on November 14, 2013 on Tenkai Knights' official YouTube channel.
