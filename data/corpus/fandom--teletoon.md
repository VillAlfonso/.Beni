Teletoon (portmanteau of "television" and "cartoon") (stylized as TELETOON) is an English broacaster in Canada, owned by Astral Media and Corus Entertainment, that airs animated series and runs on Eastern and Pacific schedules. The channel was licensed in 1996 by the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission (CRTC) and was launched on October 17, 1997. This channel does not have any connection with the French broadcaster of the same name and is known for broadcasting many famous shows and series like Total Drama, 6teen, Stoked, Bakugan, Thundercats, and Transformers. During the day, the shows are targeted for children and young teen audiences. At night, the shows are focused towards old teen to adult audiences.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

Teletoon aired the first episode of Tenkai Knights, along with 2, 3 , and 4, on September 28, 2013. New episodes air every Saturday at 7:30am. When Cartoon Network took breaks, Teletoon was still airing new episodes.
