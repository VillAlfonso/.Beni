Tenkai Knights Keyrings (Korean: 텐카이나이트 열쇠 고리, Tenkai Naiteu Yeolsoe Goli), also called Tenkai Knights Keychains, are keyrings that were released under the Tenkai Knights product line in 2014 for the western world and in early 2015 for Korea.
