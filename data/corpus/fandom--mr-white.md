Mr. White (Japanese: ホワイト, Howaito, Korean: 화이트, Hwaiteu, Hebrew: מר וויט) is a supporting character in Tenkai Knights. He is an elderly man who owns the Shop of Wonders (home to the Transdimensional Portal), which he admits in "Two Worlds", is not very successful, since it didn't have any customers for months.

He is voiced by Brian Beacock in English and by Keiji Fujiwara in Japanese.

## Appearance

His trademark style is and old-fashoined dull red millitary cap, with a yellow bandana and white coat. He also wears glasses. 55 years old.

## Personality

He has a very secretive and mysterious personality.
