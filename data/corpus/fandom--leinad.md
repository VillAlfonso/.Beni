Leinad (Japanese: スペクトロス・レッドウォ―リアー, Supekutorosu Reddo Woriā, Spectros Red Warrior, Korean: 레드워리어, Redeu Worieo, Hebrew: ליינד) is a white and red Corekai soldier.

## Trivia

<!--ep:17-->
- There is also a toy of him, that when combined with Kutor, Tributon, and Bravenwolf can form the Protojet. Most red Corekai soldiers seem to be named Leinad, however, few are ever adressed.

## In the Series

<!--ep:11-->
Leinad can be seen talking to Beag near the beginning of the episode "Two Sides to Every Coin".
