Tenkai Knights Season 2 is the second season of Tenkai Knights. It hasn't aired anywhere yet and is unlikely to come.

## Development

The second season of Tenkai Knights was unoffcially announced on Facebook when the series' official account responded to a fan question. However as the time went by it became obvious, that Season 2 won't come, as Tenkai Knights wasn't mentioned in any presentations by Spin Master since 2015. Instead Spin Master announced that they will reboot Bakugan. Thus it is likely that Spin Master cancelled Tenkai Knights in favor of Bakugan and if Tenkai Knights ever comes back it won't be a lot more than a filler line for the time Bakugan isn't airing.

## Trivia

- Although it was announced on July 01, 2014 and once again confirmed in December of the same year, there is still no news about it.
- On June 13, 2015, a member of the dedicated Facebook group Tenkai Knights Forever posted that Season 2 will air in 2016. She reportedly knows someone who is friends with Bryce Papenbrook.
- As of 2018, Season 2 has yet to air nor be confirmed as cancelled by Cartoon Network or Spin Master. This caused many supporters of the series over the past years even to this day to believe in the idea that Season 2 would never come. Despite all of this, the Spin Master website for the series and its page on the Cartoon Network website are still accessible.
