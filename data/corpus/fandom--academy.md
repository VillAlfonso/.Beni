Academy (Korean: 아카데미, Akademi), fully addressed as Academy Plastic Model Co., Ltd (Korean: 아카데미 과학 주식회사, Akademi Gwahag Jusighoesa, Eng. Translated: Academy of Sciences Corporation), is a Korean company that specializes in making plastic models and toys.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

In Korea, Academy is the company that makes the toys for Tenkai Knights.
