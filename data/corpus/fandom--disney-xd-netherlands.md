Disney XD is a Dutch broadcast channel that broadcasts shows in the Netherlands and in Flanders, Belgium. Their headquarters is located in Hilversum, Netherlands. On August 2, 1997, the channel was launched as Fox Kids. Jetix replaced Fox Kids on February 13, 2005. On January 1, 2010, Jetix was replaced by Disney XD. The channel is on for 12 hours per day, since it time-shares with another Dutch station called Veronica. In The Netherlands, it is possible to watch a 24 hour version digitally by the operators of Ziggo and UPC Netherlands. Shows on the channel are in English with Dutch subtitles or are dubbed in Dutch, except for productions in Flemish and Dutch. There are some anime shows on Disney XD like Pokémon, Sonic X, Inazuma Eleven, and B-Daman.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

There is currently no information known about Tenkai Knights airing on Disney XD, except that it aired on May 2014.
