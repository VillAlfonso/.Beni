The Best (Italian: Il Migliore, Hebrew: הטוב ביותר, Spanish: El Major, Dutch: De Besten, Turkish: En İyi, French: Le Meilleur, German: Der Beste) is the third webisode of Tenkai Knights: Origins. It was first streamed in the United States on October 02, 2013 on Tenkai Knights' official YouTube channel.

## Plot

Vilius is still convinced that he has to be the best Tenkai Knight and yet again challenges the Knights to a battle. As soon as Vilius attacks, Bravenwolf leaps in to protect the other Knights. Bravenwolf eventually knocks Vilius down but Vilius throws Bravenwolf across the arena and drains his Tenkai Energy. The Guardians revive Bravenwolf and tell Vilius that Tenkai Energy Drain is strictly forbidden. Vilius then is stripped of Tenkai Knighthood. At the end, Vilius's eyes glow red, indicating that he is now Corrupted.
