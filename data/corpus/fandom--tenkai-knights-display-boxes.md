Tenkai Knights Display Boxes (Japanese: テンカイナイトディスプレイボックス, Tenkai Naito Disupurei Bokkusu, Korean: 텐카이나이트 디스플레이 박스, Tenkai Naiteu Diseupeullei Bagseu) are boxes that are used to display single packs and mystery packs. Depending on the wave and mystery pack series, each display box does look different.
