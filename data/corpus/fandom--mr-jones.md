Mr. Jones (Japanese: セイランのパパ, Seiran no papa, Mr. Washizaki, Korean: 세이란 아빠, Seiran appa, Hebrew: מר ג'ונס) is a minor character in Tenkai Knights. He is also Ceylan's father. He appears in "The Power of Four", "Toxsa 2.0", and "Corrupted Earth".

His voice actor is currently unknown in English and is voiced by Hiroshi Tsuchida in Japanese.

## Ceylan Jones

Ceylan is his son. They appear to have the same hairstyle like Guren and his dad.
