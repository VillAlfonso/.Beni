Vilius (Japanese: ヴィリウス, Viriusu) is a toy released under the Tenkai Knights toy line by Spin Master, Happinet, and Academy.

## Western Edition

On the back of Vilius' North American box, it says "Vilius, the evil leader".

## Description

Vilius, the evil leader of the Corrupted army fights against the Tenkai Knights for control of Quarton. Shapeshift from brick to robot and build his armor and shield. Spearhead into battle unleashing his deadly Tenkai Trident. And when the evil Tenkai energy of one isn’t enough, unlock even more Tenkai energy by calling on the combined power of Vilius, Sho, Rho (Shadius), and Hos. Shift and combine each character to create Robofusion and soar into battle as the evil Xenoship! Build your Tenkai army and discover an entire world of shapeshifting bricks: humanoid, winged, 4-legged, action, titan, power, and weapon bricks. Each one contains its own unique shapeshifting power and is compatible with leading construction brands. Collect them all to unleash your Tenkai energy. Recreate epic battles from the Tenkai Knights anime series. Only you can master your Tenkai energy to defeat Vilius and his Corrupted army! IONIX – the revolutionary line of never before seen construction bricks that change their shape before your eyes! These construction bricks shapeshift and change to become mini figures, action figures, weapons, gear, gadgets, and more. What's more, our bricks snap together with other leading construction systems. IONIX – Shapeshift, build, unleash!

## Features

- Bricks like nothing you’ve seen before. Bricks that come alive. Bricks that shapeshift. Bricks with unexpected surprises. Bricks that unlock your imagination.
- Build Vilius, the evil leader of the Corrupted army into a fully articulated figure.
- Arm and unleash his Tenkai power using his armor, shield, and deadly Tenkai Trident!
- Collect Vilius 10005, Sho 10006, Hos 10007, and Rho (Shadius) 10008 to build the evil Robofusion Xenoship. Downloadable instructions at www.tenkaiknights.com.
- Build your Tenkai power by collecting the entire world of shapeshifting bricks including: humanoid, winged, 4-legged, action, titan, power, and weapon bricks!
- Fully compatible with leading construction brands. Made for boys aged 6-14.
