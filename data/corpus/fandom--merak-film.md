Merak Film, fully called Merak Film S.r.l, is a Italian company, founded in 1980, that specializes in dubbing for anime, sitcoms, movies, cartoons, and more.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

Merak Film makes the Italian dub for Tenkai Knights.
