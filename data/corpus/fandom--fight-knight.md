'(Japanese (Katakana): 分裂！テンカイナイト) is the thirty-third episode of Tenkai Knights. It first aired on April 20, 2014 on Teletoon in Canada.

## Summary

<!--ep:33-->
When the gang try a whack-a-mole style practice exercise inside a large room where snakes pop out in every direction, Ceylan manages to beat Chooki's score.

## In the episode

<!--ep:33-->
- Granox and Slyger were both sent to Earth.
- Due to a technical error, when the Knights were activated and about to be sent to Quarton, their original forms were shown.
- Lydendor was the third Knight to unlock Elemental Titan Mode.
- Valorn was knocked back before he could finish giving the command for Elemental Titan Mode in this episode.
- This is the third episode in which Guardian Megafusion appears.
- Bravenwolf tried going into Elemental Titan Mode, but for some unknown reason, failed.
- Guardian Notus was the 3rd guardian to use guardian tenkai megafusion.
