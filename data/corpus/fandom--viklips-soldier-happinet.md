Viklips Soldier (Japanese: ヴィクリプス・ソルジヤー, Vikuripusu Sorujiyā) is a Basic Figure released under the Tenkai Knights Japanese toy line. It was released by Happinet on April 5, 2014 in Japan. It was included with Tributon in the 2-in-1 Volt Jet/ Sky Griffin pack for 3,758円.
