Bandai Namco (Japanese: バンダイナムコ, Bandai Namuko), fully adressed as Bandai Namco Holdings Inc. (BNHD) (Japanese: 株式会社バンダイナムコホールディングス, Kabushiki Gaisha Bandai Namuko Hōrudingusu) and also called the Bandai Namco Group, is a Japanese holding company that was from Bandai's and Namco's merge on September 29, 2005 in Tokyo, Japan. Their headquarters is located in Shinagawa, Tokyo, and their industry is based on video games, amusement parks, toys, and anime. Shukuo Ishikawa is the current president and CEO (Chief Executive Officer) of Bandai Namco. Bandai Namco's services are worldwide.

## Involvement in Tenkai Knights

On January 30, 2014, Bandai Namco announced that Tenkai Knights: Brave Battle, the first Tenkai Knights video game, to release on the Nintendo 3DS in Japan on September 25, 2014, in Europe in Fall 2014, and in North America in Summer 2014.
