Anime News Network (Japanese: アニメニュースネットワーク, Anime Nyūsu Nettowāku, Chinese: 動畫新聞網, Dònghuà Xīnwén Wǎng, Korean: 애니메 뉴스 네트워크, Aenime Nyuseu Neteuwokeu), or ANN for short, is a website, founded in July 1998, that specifies on reporting news of manga, Japanese popular music, video games, anime, and more within Japan, North America, and Australia. It is available in English.

## Involvement with Tenkai Knights

ANN has a page on Tenkai Knights, which shows most of the dubs that were made and most of the people and companies that were involved within those dubs.
