B-003: Spectros Red Warrior (Japanese: スペクトロス・レッドウォ―リアー, Supekutorosu Reddo Woriā) is a Basic Figure released under the Tenkai Knights Japanese toy line. It was released by Happinet on April 5, 2014 in Japan for 734円. A special variant of Spectros Red Warrior was released with Kurokishi in the 2-in-1 BlasTank/ War Stallion pack.
