'(Japanese (Katakana): 迫りくるスライガー) is the twenty-eighth episode of Tenkai Knights. It first aired on March 16, 2014 on Teletoon in Canada.

## Summary

<!--ep:28-->
The Tenkai Knights discover the energy they absorbed has altered their appearances, granting them new powers. In the meantime, Vilius uses the Guardians' portal to send Slyger to Earth in order to find and destroy the Knights in their human forms.

## Plot

<!--ep:28-->
After the Knights' victory against Granox, Slyger, in his humanoid robot form, confronts and chases Guren. While he, Chooki, and Ceylan are running, Toxsa gets a device that can be used to send Slyger back to Quarton. They succeed and fight Slyger on his planet and gain another victory. In the end, Guren pointed out that what Slyger said was right, which was that nowhere's safe anymore.

## In the episode

<!--ep:28-->
- Valorn was the second Knight to use his elemental attack.
- Slyger makes his first earth appearance.
- Valorn defeats Slyger's Tenkai titan mode with his elemental special move.

## Regional Differences

<!--ep:28-->
- Slyger does not scan the pedestrian's tuxedo. He grabs him between the buildings and steals his clothes.
- The scene involving Mr. White having a flashback with Boreas was made up of reused scenes in the English dub. In the Japanese dub, it was replaced with a scene where Mr. White called Guren's dad to update him about Slyger coming to Earth.
- A scene is added where Guren called his dad.
- The Portal Weapon is originally a device invented on Earth rather than left to Mr. White from Boreas.
