Quartonians are the race of the Tenkai, the Corrupted mechabeasts, and the Corekai vehicles, that live on Quarton. They are a species of shapeshifting robots, beasts, and vehicles, disguised as toy bricks. They are all known for their ability to generate Tenkai energy, the most valuable source in the universe.
