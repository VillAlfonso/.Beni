Ms. Fenwick (Hebrew: גברת פינוויק) is a minor character in Tenkai Knights. She is a history teacher of many unnamed students, including Guren, Ceylan, and Gen.

She is voiced by Colleen O'Shaughnessey in English and by Michiko Neya in Japanese.

## Appearance

Ms. Finwick is a young school teacher with short brown hair and brown eyes, she also wears a pink dress with a blue bowtie around her neck and pearl earrings.39 years old.

## Guren Nash

So far she and Guren nevered had problems with each other. Guren is a good and nice student in her class.

## Ceylan Jones

<!--ep:2-->
Although she is nice to all her students in the class, she doesn't want Ceylan making trouble as revealed in "Tenkai Dragon Cube". When Ceylan shows his troublesome behavior, for example by throwing paper airplanes at new students, he only wants them to laugh and not embarrass them.

As showed in the series she is nice to Gen.
