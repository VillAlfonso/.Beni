The third DVD volume was released within the first DVD box on August 02, 2014. It contains three episodes and shows Chooki on its packing.

## Tributon Extreme (セイラン、決死の戦い)

A mysterious new student arrives in Benham City while on Quarton, Tributon fights to achieve Tenkai Titan mode. In the end, the Tenkai Knights face the appearance of Dromus, a doppleganger of Bravenwolf.

## Chooki's Mojo (運をとりもどせ！)

Chooki has always been good at sports, school, and, well, everything. But in order to save Toxsa, Chooki gets himself jinxed and becomes clumsy and unable to do what he's best at. The four travel back to Quarton, where Chooki unlocks his Tenkai Titan mode while overcoming the jinx.

## Valley of Secrets (ビーグの導き)

<!--ep:17-->
Commander Beag sacrifices himself the save the Tenkai Knights from falling down into a canyon, causing them to develop a new Robofusion. They soon find out Beag is trapped in the Valley of Secrets and go to rescue him. They also find the fifth and last Dragon Cube inside the Valley.
