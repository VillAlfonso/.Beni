Tenkai Knights is a Japanese-Canadian anime series, commissioned by Shogakukan-Shueisha Productions and Spin Master.

It currently consists of two seasons.

## Plot

Saving the world is serious business and should never be taken lightly. It is only for the brave of heart. Guren, Ceylan, Chooki, and Toxsa were not planning on being heroes when they accidentally stumbled into an inter-dimensional portal. Ripped through space and time, they reemerged on another world as Bravenwolf, Tributon, Lydendor, and Valorn… the legendary Tenkai Knights!

## Season 1

Tenkai Knights was leaked through the Japanese anime subcontracting studio Wanpack in May 2013. One month later, it turned out to be the new project by Spin Master, who previously had been very successful with Bakugan, but failed with Redakai. It was described as a follow-up to Bakugan.

## Season 2
