Tenkai Knights Custom Pillowcases (Japanese: テンカイナイトカスタム枕カバー, Tenkai Naito Kasutamu Makurakabā, Korean: 텐카이나이트 맞춤 베개커버, Tenkai Naiteu Majchum Begaekeobeo) are custom pillowcases, made by Akajare, that were released under the Tenkai Knights product line in 2015.

## Official Description

Want a personalized specialist pillow to decorate your house? Welcome to our store, and contact us if you have your own ideas to design the pillowcase. Our fashionable decorative custom pillowcase/pillow sham is made of quality 50% cotton, 50% polyester, machine washable, colorfast, and shrink-proof. Note: Pillow case only, not include pillow inner. It's one piece. If you want a pair, please buy 2 pieces of bed pillow case/throw pillow case. Design your own style and show your personality. Just one picture is needed. Contact us now!
