The Battle for Quarton (Italian: La Battaglia per Quarton, Hebrew: הקרב על קווארטון, Spanish: La Batalla por Quarton, Dutch: De Strijd om Quarton, Turkish: Quarton için Savaş 3D, French: La Bataille de Quarton, German: Der Kampf um Quarton) is a Tenkai Knights online game that's also available as an app.

## English

The Guardians have called upon the legendary Tenkai Knights to save Quarton from Vilius. Play as Bravenwolf, Tributon, Valorn, and Lydendor to defeat Vilius and his army to save Quarton.

## How to Play

- X (Key) - Attack/Grabbing/Throwing (Push X key when in front of an object to Grab/Throw)
- Z (Key) - Dash Attack
- Space bar (Key) - Uppercut
- Arrow Keys - Move
Certain attacks (dashing or using an uppercut) require the player to use some of their Tenkai Energy (as shown on the Energy bar). The bar will recharge over time after being depleted from an attack. Uppercuts require a full bar of energy and will completely deplete it, while dashing only requires a little energy.

While playing as Tributon, holding down the X key will result in blue light, symbolizing Tributon's arrows are charging. When this is complete, the player can release the X key for a charged attack, resulting in mass damage to the enemies.
- Pickups
- Green Block (Health) - Repairs the damage you received from the enemy
- Red Block (Power) - Doubles the damage you do on opponents for a short time
- Blue Block (Energy) - Gives you infinite energy for a small amount of time
