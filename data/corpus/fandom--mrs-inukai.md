Mrs. Inukai (Japanese: ゲンのママ, Gen no mama, Korean: 겐의 엄마, Gen-ui eomma) is a minor character in Tenkai Knights. She is Gen's mother. It was revealed that in the Japanese version of Tenkai Knights, she left her husband (Mr. Inukai). It's unknown of what happened to Mrs. Inukai. She could be dead or alive. She can be seen in a picture on the right side of a wall that was shown in Gen's past in "The Dark Unlock".

## Gen

Mrs. Inukai's son. They share the same hair color.
